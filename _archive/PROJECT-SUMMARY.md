## Key Insights & Recommendations

### Unique Competitive Advantages (Undermarketed)
1. **I-35 Interstate Node** — El Dorado has direct exits (71/76). Not a bypass. This is a major economic asset for logistics, manufacturing, FDI.
2. **FTZ #28 Enrollment** — Butler County already enrolled. Customs duty savings can be worth millions for mega-site tenants. Most site selectors don't know about this.
3. **Unique Water Rights** — Only Kansas city to own water rights (contract with USACE). 23M gal/day + 8.5B gal/yr additional capacity. Data center recruitment candidate.
4. **#1 State Park** — El Dorado State Park gets 1M+ visitors/year, "by a landslide" more than #2. Zero lakeside dining = massive unmet demand. 26-venue strategy addresses this.
5. **AMAZONE 2025** — Proof of concept. German company chose El Dorado over other US locations. Story to tell international recruitment prospects.

### Critical Path Items for Phase 1
1. **Regional Water Authority** — Formalize El Dorado + Butler County partnership. Unlock water export revenue + industrial recruitment.
2. **SS4A Project Execution** — $10M+ federal safety grant already approved. Near-term wins build momentum, prove competence for larger grants.
3. **Butler County Broadband Spine** — $166.6M BEAD allocation approved (Dec. 2025). Critical for data center recruitment, remote worker attraction, tech jobs.
4. **Grant Application Pipeline** — $200M+ federal programs eligible. Prioritize: EDA Good Jobs Challenge ($25M), USDA REAP (renewable energy), HUD CDBG, EPA Brownfields, DOT BUILD.
5. **FDI Recruitment** — Model AMAZONE success. Target: Japan (JETRO), Germany (GTAI), South Korea (KOTRA). SelectUSA provides free support.

### Funding Strategy (Not Just Grants)
- **Federal/State Grants: $300M** — Won't cover everything. Competitive. Requires strong applications.
- **Municipal Bonds: $150M** — GO bonds, revenue bonds, special assessment. Depends on credit rating, political will.
- **Private Development: $400M+** — Real estate (residential, commercial), business expansion, LIHTC/HTC stacks. This is where most capital comes from.
- **FDI + EB-5: $50M+** — International investment. Mega-site, senior care campus, boutique hotel candidates.
- **Philanthropic: $10M+** — Downtown improvements, park upgrades, arts center, educational initiatives.
