## CONTINGENCY TIMELINES

### If Housing Pilot Delayed (say, 6 months)
- Ground breaking moved to Aug 2027 (instead of Feb)
- Housing occupancy moved to Apr 2028 (instead of Oct 2027)
- Housing pipeline compressed (second project starts immediately after pilot approved)
- Mitigation: Faster developer responses, parallel financing negotiations

### If Workforce Program Enrollment Low (Year 1)
- Extend recruitment through Q4 2026
- Add employer scholarships to incentivize enrollment
- Target older workers (mid-career changers, not just high school grads)
- Mitigation: Program still launches on schedule, enrollment catches up by cohort #2

### If Employer Hiring Freezes (Economic Downturn)
- Shift focus to upskilling current workforce (incumbent worker training)
- Extend job placement timeline (30 days → 60–90 days)
- Bridge funding for graduates waiting for jobs (city provides stipend)
- Mitigation: Program continues, job market improves eventually
