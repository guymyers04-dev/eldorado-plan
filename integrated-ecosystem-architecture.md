Q: What is Vision 2040?
A: Vision 2040 is a digital platform connecting business leaders, investors, young professionals, students, and community members around shared economic growth goals for El Dorado.

Q: Why is it called "Vision 2040"?
A: We're building toward 2040 with a long-term perspective on sustainable growth, opportunity, and community belonging.

Q: Who created Vision 2040?
A: Vision 2040 is a collaborative initiative of the City of El Dorado, Chamber of Commerce, Main Street El Dorado, USD 490, Butler Community College, and 160+ nonprofit and civic organizations.

Q: Is Vision 2040 a government project?
A: It's a partnership between city government and community organizations. Everyone leads it together.

Q: When will Vision 2040 launch publicly?
A: The platform launches publicly in November 2026. July-October is our community activation and beta testing phase.

A: Three ways: (1) Become an Ambassador, (2) Sponsor an Event, (3) Business Development Partnership. Learn more: [/get-involved]

A: It's completely free. We provide all materials and training.

A: About 30 minutes per month, though you can do more if you want to. Includes optional monthly training call + sharing content whenever it fits your schedule.

Q: Can I be an ambassador if I don't have a business?
A: Absolutely! We're looking for community leaders of all kinds: nonprofit directors, civic leaders, educators, parents, students, anyone passionate about El Dorado.

Q: What if I want to do more than one thing (ambassador + sponsor)?
