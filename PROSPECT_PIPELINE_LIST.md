## COLD CONTACT MANAGEMENT

### Best Practices for Initial Outreach:

1. **Lead with data advantage** (water for data centers; I-35 for manufacturing; lake for hospitality)
2. **Reference comparable** (AMAZONE for FDI; Craft Hotels for boutique; KOA for RV parks)
3. **Quantify ROI** (financial model for operator; tax benefits for city partnership)
4. **Propose next step** (15-min call → site visit → proposal → LOI)
5. **Track engagement** (monthly scorecard; escalate to CEO if Stage 2+)

### Sales Cycle Stages:

| Stage | Definition | Timeline | Actions |
|-------|-----------|----------|---------|
| **1: Initial Contact** | Exec summary sent; awaiting response | Month 1 | Follow-up in 2 weeks if no response |
| **2: Engagement** | Live conversation; prospect interested | Month 2–3 | Schedule site visit; send detailed proposal |
| **3: Due Diligence** | Proposal review; feasibility study | Month 4–6 | Answer RFIs; facilitate technical calls |
| **4: Negotiation** | LOI drafted; material terms discussed | Month 7–9 | Legal review; SB 98 package finalized |
| **5: LOI Signed** | Agreement signed; construction planning | Month 10–12 | Groundbreaking; financing close |
