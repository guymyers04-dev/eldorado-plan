Frequently Asked Questions

About Vision 2040

What is Vision 2040? +

Vision 2040 is a digital platform connecting business leaders, investors, young professionals, students, and community members around shared goals of economic growth, opportunity, and community belonging. It's built BY El Dorado, FOR El Dorado, uniting our city's 1,559 Chamber businesses, 2,000+ students, and 160+ nonprofit organizations into one coordinated ecosystem.

Why is it called "Vision 2040"? +

We're building toward 2040 with a long-term perspective on sustainable economic growth, creating opportunities for all, and fostering community belonging. Vision 2040 represents our shared aspiration for El Dorado's future.

Who created Vision 2040? +

Vision 2040 is a collaborative initiative of the City of El Dorado, El Dorado Chamber of Commerce, Main Street El Dorado, USD 490, Butler Community College, and 160+ nonprofit and civic organizations. It's truly a community effort.

Is Vision 2040 a government project? +

It's a partnership between city government and community organizations. Everyone leads it together. It's not a top-down government initiative—it's a grassroots movement supported by city resources.

When will Vision 2040 launch publicly? +

The platform launches publicly in November 2026. July–October is our community activation and beta testing phase. Events start July 1 with the Chamber Chat pitch.

Getting Involved

How do I get involved in Vision 2040? +

Three ways: (1) Become an Ambassador—share Vision 2040 with your network (30 min/month), (2) Sponsor an Event—support community growth while building your brand ($250–$1,500), (3) Business Development Partnership—lead recruitment in your sector or industry. Learn more on our Get Involved page.

What does it cost to be an ambassador? +

It's completely free! We provide all materials, training, and support. The only cost is if you decide to sponsor an event in addition to being an ambassador.

How much time does being an ambassador really take? +

About 30 minutes per month on average. This includes optional monthly training calls and sharing content whenever it fits your schedule. You can do more if you want to, but 30 minutes/month is the baseline commitment.

Can I be an ambassador if I don't have a business? +

Absolutely! We're looking for community leaders of all kinds: nonprofit directors, civic leaders, educators, parents, students, young professionals, and anyone passionate about El Dorado's future. All backgrounds welcome.

What if I want to do more than one thing? +

Great idea! Many people are ambassadors AND sponsors. You can do as much or as little as you want. Check out the Ambassador, Sponsor, and Get Involved pages for all your options.

Events & Activities

When are the Vision 2040 events? +

Main events in July 2026: Chamber Chat (Jul 1), July 4 Independence Day Booth, Butler County Fair (Jul 16–21), Farmers Markets (bi-weekly), First Fridays (monthly). See our full calendar for details and registration.

Can I bring my kids to the events? +

Yes! Most events are family-friendly. July 4 and County Fair especially. Check individual event pages for specifics about activities for kids.

Do I need to register for events? +

Registration is encouraged but not required for most events. Some may require advance signup for planning purposes. Check individual event pages for details.

Can I volunteer at the events? +

Yes! We need 80–120 volunteers across July events. Whether you can help for one hour or one full day, we'd love your support. Sign-up coming soon on the Events page.

Sponsorship

How much does it cost to sponsor an event? +

Sponsorship ranges from $250–$1,500 depending on the event and sponsorship level. Options include: July 4 Booth ($250–$500), County Fair ($750–$1,500), First Fridays ($250–$500). See the Sponsor page for full details.

What do sponsors get in return? +

Logo placement, social media coverage, networking opportunities, and community recognition. Benefits increase with sponsorship level. See the Sponsor page for a complete benefits comparison.

Can we sponsor more than one event? +

Yes! Many sponsors support multiple events. Contact us for multi-event packages and custom arrangements at info@eldorado.gov or (316) 321-9100.

How do I become a sponsor? +

Fill out the sponsorship inquiry form on the Sponsor page. We'll follow up within 24 hours with details and next steps.

Other Questions

What are the benefits of Vision 2040 for businesses? +

Market intelligence (real-time job forecasts, property pipeline), investor access (deal flow, ROI analysis), talent pipeline (direct access to 2,000+ students), network amplification (reach 1,559+ Chamber members), and more. Learn more on our About page.

What about students and young professionals? +

Vision 2040 offers career pathways, skills marketplace, internship opportunities, and networking events. Students can explore opportunities in El Dorado. Young professionals can find jobs, mentorship, and community. Check out our Community pages when the platform launches in November.
