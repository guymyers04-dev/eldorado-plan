Our Foundation

El Dorado — Ready to Lead Again

El Dorado has always punched above its weight. In 1915, the Stapleton No. 1 oil discovery — made using scientific geologic mapping — sparked the first oil boom in Kansas. Within 3 years, 9% of all U.S. oil came from El Dorado. That same entrepreneurial grit, combined with an 8,400-acre lake, the 2nd-largest community college in Kansas, and a location 29 miles from Wichita's city center, makes El Dorado uniquely positioned for a 21st-century resurgence.

This plan is not a wish list. It is a prioritized, phased, and financially grounded roadmap built on assets that already exist and advantages that no comparable Kansas city can match simultaneously.
