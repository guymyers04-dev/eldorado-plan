# El Dorado, Kansas — Niche Partnership Opportunities Research
**June 2026 | Comprehensive Market Intelligence Report**

---

## Executive Summary

This report identifies high-potential partnership and recruitment opportunities for El Dorado, KS across 10 strategic categories, moving beyond anchor institutions. The research reveals **specific company expansion plans, regional contact information, and tactical outreach strategies** for 2025-2026.

**Key Findings:**
- **Data Centers:** Rural Kansas becoming a top-tier location due to SB 98 (20-year tax exemption), power/water availability, and AI/ML demand
- **Senior Living:** Explosive growth in rural assisted living, with Sunrise, Brookdale, and smaller chains expanding nationally
- **Agricultural Tech:** Consolidation around precision agriculture, equipment manufacturing, and software platforms
- **International FDI:** JETRO (Chicago), GTAI (Chicago), KOTRA (Chicago) all actively recruiting for Midwest; AMAZONE proof-of-concept validates El Dorado model
- **EB-5 Visa:** Kansas Regional Center active; senior living and hotels are strongest candidates for $800K minimum investment
- **Film Production:** Kansas lacks incentive program (unlike 40+ states), but proposed 2025 bill includes 10% rural designation
- **Venture Capital:** Midwest angel networks and accelerators (gBETA, Gener8tor, InvestMidwest) targeting rural startups

## 1. DATA CENTER & TECH INFRASTRUCTURE PARTNERS

### Current Market Dynamics

**Kansas Competitive Position:**
- **SB 98 Data Center Sales Tax Exemption** (signed April 2025, effective July 2025): 20-year state + local sales tax exemption for projects $250M+ creating permanent jobs
- **BEAD Broadband:** Kansas approved $166.6M federal funding (Dec. 2025) with 14 subrecipients; 30% fiber, 67% fixed wireless, 3% LEO satellite
- **Power & Water:** El Dorado owns unique water rights (23M gal/day), connected to HF Sinclair refinery grid, growing interest from hyperscale operators
- **Trend:** Rural Kansas shifting from "secondary location" to "primary location" for AI/ML data centers (2025-2026)

### Film & Production

**Critical Opportunity — Kansas Incentive Program Gap:**

**Status:** Kansas one of few states WITHOUT film incentive program (unlike 40+ states)

**Proposed Solution (2025 Bill):**
- Would create Kansas Film and Digital Media Production Development Program
- Up to $10M tax credits per year
- **Key Detail:** Minimum 10% of credits reserved for rural productions
- Bill passed House 102-22, Senate 32-5; caught in Gov. Kelly's 2024 veto

**Neighboring Context:**
- Missouri: 5% base tax credit; additional 5% if 15%+ of production in rural/blighted area
- Excellent targeting for El Dorado lake/landscape for low-budget indie films, commercials, web series

**Contact Strategy:**
1. **Kansas Department of Commerce**
   - Film incentive program revival; monitor 2026 legislative session
2. **Kansas Governor's Office**
   - Advocacy for 2026 bill reintroduction
3. **Kansas Film Commission (proposed)**
   - Not yet established; opportunity to shape program design
