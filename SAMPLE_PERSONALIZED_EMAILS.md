## KEY LEARNINGS (APPLY TO ALL 35 CONTACTS)

### 1. Research Pattern
For each company, spend **10 minutes researching:**
```
☐ Recent news / funding / expansion announcements
☐ Key products or services (what they're known for)
☐ Company strategic direction (from LinkedIn, website)
☐ Decision-maker LinkedIn profile (shows they're real)
☐ 2-3 specific details to reference in email
```

### 2. Personalization Levels

**MINIMUM (Acceptable):**
- Company name + decision-maker first name used
- 1 specific product/service mentioned
- 1 company-specific benefit called out

**GOOD (Recommended):**
- 3-4 company-specific details
- 1-2 references to their strategic focus
- Email shows 15+ minutes of research

**EXCELLENT (What we did above):**
- 5+ company-specific details
- Mentions their growth/expansion signals
- References their customer base, products, and strategic goals
- Feels like you know their business

### 3. UTM Parameter Pattern

**Email 1A:** `...&utm_content=email_1a_cold&utm_term=[company_name]`  
**Email 1B:** `...&utm_content=email_1b_followup&utm_term=[company_name]`  
**Email 1C:** No link (warm intro request)

**Why:** Tracks which company you're reaching out to + which sequence stage

### 4. Timing Pattern

**Climate FieldView schedule (applies to all Tier 1 targets):**
- Day 1 (Monday 8:25 AM): Email 1A (cold)
- Day 8 (Next Monday 8:00 AM): Email 1B (follow-up)
- Day 15 (Following Monday 8:00 AM): Email 1C (warm intro)

**If response received:** Skip to calendar meeting, LOI negotiation
