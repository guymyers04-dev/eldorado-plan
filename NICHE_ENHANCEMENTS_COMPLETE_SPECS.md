### FINANCE SECTION

#### F1: "My Tax Impact" Calculator
**Purpose:** Answer "Will Vision 2040 cost me more in taxes?" with honest projections and scenario analysis

**Core Features:**
- **Step 1:** Your situation (home value, income, business owner?, length of stay)
- **Step 2:** Current tax burden (property tax, city income tax, sales tax, school tax, county tax, total % of income)
- **Step 3:** Scenario selection:
  - Baseline: historical growth rate (~1% annually), taxes grow with inflation only
  - Growth: optimistic Vision 2040 (4% annual home value growth, 1-2% tax rate increase, 2-3% wage growth)
  - Conservative: partial success (2% growth, 0.5% tax rate increase, 1% wage growth)
  - Recession: economic downturn (home value decline, taxes stable, wages stagnant)
- **Step 4:** Projections table (2024, 2026, 2030, 2035, 2040) showing: home value, tax rate, annual tax payment, % of income, regional comparison
- **Step 5:** Policy levers explanation (TIF, grants, public-private partnerships, efficiency improvements, tax rate increases)
- **Step 6:** Personal impact summary
- **Share & Export:** Personalized PDF report, share scenarios, save to compare over time

**Data Requirements:**
- Current property tax rates
- Historical home value appreciation
- Wage growth data
- Vision 2040 economic projections
- TIF/tax policy details
- Grant forecasts

**Integration Points:**
- finance.html core
- Economy pages
