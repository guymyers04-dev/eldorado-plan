# El Dorado, KS — Niche & Emerging Industries Research
## Comprehensive Industry Targeting with Contact Information & Execution Plans

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

El Dorado's non-replicable advantages (water, I-35 node, AMAZONE momentum, FTZ enrollment, skilled workforce) position it uniquely for 5 emerging/niche sectors beyond traditional data center + FDI plays:

1. **Precision Agriculture Technology** (AI sensors, autonomous equipment, data services)
2. **Advanced Battery & EV Supply Chain** (lithium processing, thermal mgmt, assembly)
3. **Biotech & AgriGenomics** (crop genetics, probiotics, bio-inputs)
4. **Specialty Manufacturing for Aerospace** (composites, fasteners, precision parts)
5. **Logistics & Cold Chain Innovation** (automated warehousing, cold storage, last-mile)
