E1

Economy: Interactive Milestone Timeline

Add an animated timeline showing North Gateway buildout, health economy phases, mega-site development in 2-5 year increments. Each milestone is clickable with: funding status, responsible agencies, private investment expected, jobs created, tax revenue impact. Helps residents visualize "when does this affect me?"

E2

Economy: "Success Story" Case Studies

Embed 3-4 mini-narratives of similar mid-size towns that executed comparable strategies (similar population, similar oil/industrial past, similar lake recreation asset). Show the timeline: what they committed to, what actually happened, what surprised them. Builds credibility through peer example rather than theory.

E3

Economy: "My Job" Calculator

Interactive tool: resident enters their current job sector (retail, health, agriculture, etc.). Tool shows: which Vision 2040 projects hire in their field, timeline of those jobs, typical salary range (compared to region), upskilling pathways if job sector is changing. Makes abstract 2040 vision personal—"here's where I might work."

H1

Housing: Affordability Tracker

Visual showing rent/home price evolution from 2024→2040. Compare current El Dorado prices to: regional averages, cities of similar size, cities that executed Vision 2040–like growth. Honest question: "Will Vision 2040 price me out?" Answer with data + policy levers (LIHTC, inclusionary zoning, community land trusts) being used to prevent it.

Housing: "Where Should I Live?" Commute Finder

Interactive map: resident enters where they work (El Dorado, Wichita, or flexible). Tool shows all neighborhoods grouped by: commute time, walkability, schools nearby, new development happening, price range. Makes abstract planning concrete—shows residents where their next home might be.

Housing: Resident Profile Case Studies

Lake: Seasonal Activity Calendar

12-month calendar showing what's happening at lake: marina opening, sailing races, fishing seasons, amphitheater concerts, glamping availability, trail conditions. Helps visitors plan trips. Link each event to new Vision 2040 infrastructure being built (new restaurant opening July 2027, new boat launch coming 2026).

Downtown: Before/After Photo Comparisons

Slider-style before/after showing current vacant storefronts side-by-side with architect renderings of rehabilitated buildings (from Historic Tax Credit applications, Main Street program). Real buildings, real plans. Shows progress is not theoretical.

Downtown: Interactive Walking Tour

Self-guided audio/text tour of downtown with: current building names/histories, vision 2040 planned changes, QR codes at each stop. Residents become familiar with the plan by walking it. Link to print/mobile versions for tourists.

Arts & Culture: Artist Opportunity Board

Curated marketplace for artists: performance venues opening, public art commission opportunities, studio space availability, grant/funding deadlines. Makes the "arts economy" vision feel real—here's where *I* perform, here's where I apply for a grant.

Education: K-12 to Career Pathways
