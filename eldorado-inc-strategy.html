El Dorado Inc — Strategic Execution Plan

How to Move from Planning to Results

Vision 2040 is the blueprint. El Dorado Inc is the vehicle. This page outlines the specific strategies to deliver impact in 12-24 months.

The Challenge

El Dorado Inc was founded to drive economic development, but has operated without a clear strategic focus, measurable goals, or accountability for results. Vision 2040 changes that. Now there's a clear, investor-grade plan. El Dorado Inc's job is to execute it — with specific targets, monthly reporting, and authority to move deals forward.

🎯 3-Year Vision for El Dorado Inc

By 2028: El Dorado Inc becomes known as a "deal-ready" economic development organization that closes projects on schedule, delivers on commitments, and attracts tier-1 corporate interest to the city.

  • ✓ 1 hyperscale anchor tenant committed (500+ jobs)
  • ✓ 200+ housing units under construction
  • ✓ Downtown 3-block retail/office activation complete
  • ✓ North Gateway infrastructure Phase 1 delivered
  • ✓ West Marina operational

Key Initiatives for El Dorado Inc

🎯

1. Hyperscale Tenant Recruitment

Goal: Secure commitment from one mega-tenant (Google, Amazon, Intel, TSMC) by 2027 representing $500M–$3B investment.

Specific Actions:

  • ✓ Hire dedicated VP of Corporate Development (Q3 2026)
  • ✓ Develop hyperscale toolkit (branding, site specs, incentive packages)
  • ✓ Register with JETRO (Japan), KOTRA (Korea), GTAI (Germany) FDI networks
  • ✓ Attend SelectUSA Summit & industry trade shows (2×/year)
  • ✓ Direct outreach to 50+ target companies (monthly cadence)
  • ✓ Site visits/tours for qualified prospects (quarterly)
Timeline
9–18 months to LOI
🏢

2. Downtown Retail Activation

Goal: Fill 3 anchor storefronts on Central Avenue + recruit 5 complementary retailers by 2027.

Specific Actions:

  • ✓ Develop retail recruitment target list (grocery, apparel, F&B)
  • ✓ Create retail incentive package (TIF, tax abatement, build-out assistance)
  • ✓ Direct outreach to regional retailers (2×/month)
  • ✓ Offer visit/demo program (downtown tour, lease negotiation support)
  • ✓ Broker relationship with building owners (facilitate lease terms)
  • ✓ Monthly reporting on prospect pipeline (targets, status, probability)
Timeline
First anchor LOI: Q2 2027
🏘️

3. Residential Development Recruitment

Goal: Recruit 2–3 major residential developers to North Gateway + secure commitments for 150+ units by 2027.

Specific Actions:

  • ✓ Develop North Gateway marketing package (master plan, utilities, zoning)
  • ✓ Create residential development incentive template (LIHTC, TIF, off-site improvements)
  • ✓ Target regional builders active in Kansas/Oklahoma/Missouri
  • ✓ Site tours + financial feasibility discussions (quarterly)
  • ✓ Broker relationships between developer, city, utilities
  • ✓ Fast-track permitting (60–90 day target)
Timeline
First anchor LOI: Q3 2026
🎢

4. Tourism & Hospitality

Goal: Open West Marina + secure boutique hotel + activate lake corridor amenities by 2027.

Specific Actions:

  • ✓ Facilitate West Marina development agreement execution (Q2 2026)
  • ✓ Recruit boutique hotel operator (35–50 rooms, Lake positioning)
  • ✓ Recruit lakefront restaurant operators (3–5 concepts)
  • ✓ Develop boat tour/rental operations
  • ✓ Coordinate park improvements + event programming
  • ✓ Marketing: position as regional destination (Wichita metro reach)
Timeline
West Marina open: Q2 2027
💼

5. Entrepreneur Support

Goal: Support 15–20 local startup & small business growth by 2027 (creation of 50+ jobs).

Specific Actions:

  • ✓ Monthly entrepreneur meetups (networking, education, mentoring)
  • ✓ Small business loan access program (SBA, Kansas loans)
  • ✓ Subsidized commercial space for first-time entrepreneurs
  • ✓ Business plan competitions + grant awards ($5K–$25K)
  • ✓ Mentorship matching program (successful business owners → founders)
  • ✓ Educational workshops (marketing, finance, operations)
Budget
$150K–250K annually
📊

6. Accountability & Reporting

Goal: Monthly public reporting on progress metrics + quarterly board updates + annual impact report.

Specific Actions:

  • ✓ Executive Director = Chief Accountable Officer for Vision 2040
  • ✓ Monthly dashboard: prospects, LOIs, construction starts, job creation
  • ✓ Quarterly board reports (actual vs. target)
  • ✓ Annual performance review linked to compensation
  • ✓ Public reporting on website + newspaper (transparency builds credibility)
  • ✓ Community town halls (yearly progress updates)
Key Metric
Jobs created + capital invested

⚡ 90-Day Sprint (Q3 2026)

First 90 days are critical. Here's what El Dorado Inc MUST accomplish to build momentum:

Week 1–2: Foundation

  • ✓ Hire VP of Corporate Development
  • ✓ Adopt Vision 2040 as official strategy
  • ✓ Create prospect CRM (Salesforce/HubSpot)
  • ✓ Develop 5 asset packages (hyperscale, residential, retail, tourism, industrial)

Week 3–4: Outreach Launch

  • ✓ Register with FDI networks (JETRO, KOTRA, GTAI)
  • ✓ Create prospect list: 30 hyperscale, 15 retailers, 10 developers
  • ✓ Launch direct outreach campaign (emails + phone calls)
  • ✓ Schedule first tour visits (target: 5 prospects by end of week 4)

Week 5–8: Build Pipeline

  • ✓ Host 3–4 prospect site visits (in-person tours)
  • ✓ Secure 2 pre-LOI discussions underway
  • ✓ Publish monthly dashboard on website
  • ✓ Attend 1 industry trade show (SelectUSA, ICSC, etc.)

Week 9–12: Results & Momentum

  • ✓ Report results: prospects, site visits, LOI progress
  • ✓ Board presentation on pipeline + next quarter targets
  • ✓ Community update on early wins
  • ✓ Plan next 90-day sprint based on learnings
