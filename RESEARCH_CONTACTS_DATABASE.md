When tracking your outreach, document:

1. **Contact Name** - Individual you're reaching out to
2. **Company/Organization** - Their employer
3. **Title** - Job title
4. **Phone** - Phone number
5. **Email** - Email address
6. **Outreach Date** - Date you first contacted them
7. **Outreach Method** - Phone / Email / LinkedIn / Warm intro
8. **Status** - Not reached / Scheduled / Completed / Follow-up needed
9. **Call Date** - When you had the call

11. **Interest Level** - High / Medium / Low / Not interested
