## CONTINGENCY & PIVOT PLANS

### If Data Center Recruitment Lags (< 3 prospects by Week 4)
- **Immediate Action:** Escalate to national brokers (not just CBRE/JLL); contact CoreWeave, Equinix directly
- **Alternative:** Shift focus to FDI acceleration; second/third FDI facilities can achieve similar job creation
- **Timeline:** Reassess by Week 6; pivot decision by Week 8

### If FDI Prospects Slow (< 5 by Week 4)
- **Immediate Action:** JETRO/GTAI/KOTRA escalation; request direct introductions to prospects
- **Alternative:** Shift to domestic manufacturers (John Deere, CLAAS US subsidiaries); similar ROI
- **Timeline:** Reassess by Week 6; expand to domestic by Week 8

### If Lake Anchors Don't Materialize (< 2 operators interested by Week 6)
- **Immediate Action:** Expand recruit beyond hotel/restaurant/RV park; target marina operator, event venue, water sports school
- **Alternative:** Reduce Phase 1 goals from 3–4 anchors to 1–2; extend timeline to Month 2–3
- **Timeline:** Reassess by Week 8; pivot by Week 10

### If Housing Financing Rejected (LIHTC application denied)
- **Immediate Action:** USDA RD becomes primary (slower but reliable); apply to additional syndicators
- **Alternative:** State programs (KHIF, KIDSBG); local nonprofit partnerships (NeighborWorks, Habitat)
- **Timeline:** Reassess by Week 8; pivot by Week 12; delay groundbreaking to Q1 2027 (still Phase 1)
