Logistics & Transportation Workforce Development

People & Education
Quick Win
$500K

Initiative Overview

What Is It?

Comprehensive Commercial Driver License (CDL) training program through USD 490, plus logistics operations certification and warehouse management training. Produces 40-50 CDL-certified drivers annually, plus warehouse/logistics supervisors.

Why It Matters

The regional logistics hub needs 150+ drivers by 2030. El Dorado can produce trained drivers locally, filling jobs immediately and keeping training revenue in community. Avg. CDL salary: $52K-65K (excellent for region).

Program Components

CDL Training Program

  • 4-week intensive classroom
  • Professional driving school partnership
  • Behind-the-wheel training (40 hrs)
  • Safety certification (CVSA)
  • DOT hazmat endorsement option
  • 40-50 graduates/year target
  • Avg. salary: $52K-65K

Logistics Operations

  • Warehouse operations (APICS)
  • Inventory management
  • Supply chain fundamentals
  • Logistics software training
  • Safety/OSHA certification
  • 20-30 graduates/year target
  • Avg. salary: $38K-48K

Supervisory Track

  • Warehouse management
  • Team leadership/communication
  • Performance management
  • Operations planning
  • Risk/compliance management
  • 10-15 completions/year target
  • Avg. salary: $48K-62K
