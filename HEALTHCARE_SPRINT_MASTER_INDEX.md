## 🎯 SPRINT AT A GLANCE

### The Vision
Transform El Dorado from a 40% healthcare spending leakage market into a $200M+ regional health hub serving all of Butler County — creating 660+ healthcare jobs, retaining $80M/year in local spending, and anchoring population growth at 18,200 by 2040.

### The Plan
$95M investment over 10 years across 4 phases:
- **Phase 1A (2025-26):** Surgical Center + Behavioral Health ($35M, 130 jobs)
- **Phase 1B (2026-27):** Telehealth + Dental Clinic ($7M, 55 jobs)
- **Phase 2 (2027-30):** Senior Campus + Wellness Zone ($38M, 240 jobs)
- **Phase 3 (2030-35):** Scale & Optimization ($15M, 100 jobs)

### The Timeline
- **Week 1 (July 1-5):** Steering committee + governance + Phase 1A roadmap
- **Weeks 2-4 (July 8-Aug 2):** USDA application + architect selection + recruitment launch
- **Weeks 5-12 (Aug 5-Oct 3):** Physician recruitment + construction docs + groundbreaking prep
- **Oct 1, 2026:** Groundbreaking ceremony (surgical center + behavioral health wing)

## 🏆 SUCCESS STORY (REFERENCE MODEL)

**Emporia, KS Case Study**
- Population: 24,000 (similar to El Dorado target)
- Strategy: Academic partnership (Newman U + Emporia State) + federal grants + physician recruitment
- Timeline: 10-year build (2015-2025)
- Result: 
  - 18 primary care physicians recruited
  - $40M surgical/behavioral health center opened (2018)
  - 28% of regional healthcare spending retained (was leaking to KC)
  - $280M cumulative health spending by 2025
  - 400+ healthcare jobs created

**El Dorado can replicate this model** with SBA Hospital anchor + USD 490/Butler CC partnership + federal grants.
