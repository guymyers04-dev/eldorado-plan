East Marina Enhancement Program

Place & Recreation
Phase 1-2
$2.5M
18 months

Initiative Overview

What Is It?

Comprehensive upgrade of existing Shady Creek Marina (100 slips, state park concession) with modern amenities, expanded capacity, and improved visitor access. Complements new West Marina by providing dual east-west waterfront anchors.

Why It Matters

Shady Creek Marina serves boaters but lacks modern amenities (electric charging, pump-out, adequate fuel). Enhancement maintains existing asset value while improving visitor experience and coordination with new West Marina.

Enhancement Scope

Current State

  • Slips: 100 (no expansion possible)
  • Fuel Dock: Basic, outdated
  • Pump-Out: Not available
  • Parking: Limited, poor condition
  • Buildings: Old, need renovation
  • Accessibility: Poor for visitors
  • Amenities: Minimal retail/services

Post-Enhancement

  • Slips: 100 maintained (modern capacity)
  • Fuel Dock: High-capacity, modern dispensers
  • Pump-Out: EPA-compliant station added
  • Parking: Expanded, improved lighting
  • Buildings: Renovated office/restrooms
  • Accessibility: ADA improvements, wayfinding
  • Amenities: Small retail (bait, tackle, supplies)

Capital Improvement Plan

Fuel Dock Modernization

Replace existing system with high-capacity modern dispensers, credit card readers, and failsafe systems.

$400K

Pump-Out Station

New EPA-compliant waste management infrastructure with proper holding and treatment systems.

$350K

Parking & Circulation

Expand parking (40 spaces), improve circulation, add ADA parking and accessible pathways.

$600K

Building Renovation

Renovate office, restrooms, and retail space. Improve HVAC, electrical, and water systems.

$500K

Dockside Infrastructure

Modern slip utilities, power pedestals, lighting, and wayfinding signage.

$400K

Amenities & Retail

Small retail space (tackle, supplies, beverages), Wi-Fi, seating areas.

$250K
Total Capital Investment $2.5M

Funding Strategy

Public Funding

  • Kansas State Parks Enhancement Grants: $800K-1M
  • City Economic Development Funds: $600-800K
  • Park District Contribution: $400-500K

Private/Operator Funding

  • KDWPT Concession Operator: $300-500K (equipment)
  • Corporate Sponsorships: $200-300K (naming rights)

Implementation Timeline

1
Planning & Design (3 months)

Phase 1: Fuel + pump-out (months 1-4). Phase 2: Parking + building (months 5-12).
