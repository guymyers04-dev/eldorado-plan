## INTRODUCTION

These email templates and call scripts are designed for immediate use by the Data Center Director, FDI Officer, Housing Consultant, and Communications Manager. Each script is customized for the target audience and includes:

- **Email Subject Line** — Professional, attention-grabbing
- **Email Body** — 3–5 paragraphs, clear value proposition, specific call to action
- **Call Script** — 60–90 second introduction for cold calling
- **Follow-Up Strategy** — When/how to follow up if no response
