Newton Intermodal Park Strategy

Critical Messaging: "Newton Intermodal Feeder Hub"

BNSF Newton Intermodal Park (opening 2025–2026, 30 miles north of El Dorado) will be a 7,000 commercial vehicle/day hub. El Dorado should position itself as the ideal secondary distribution and last-mile logistics center for freight processed through Newton. This is a powerful differentiator vs. competitors.
