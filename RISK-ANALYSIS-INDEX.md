## HOW TO USE THESE DOCUMENTS

### FOR BOARD MEMBERS
1. Read **RISK-HEAT-MAP-EXECUTIVE-SUMMARY.md** (15 min)
2. Focus on **Critical Risks section** (identifies top 4 risks + mitigation)
3. Review **Quarterly Decision Gates** (understand GO/NO-GO milestones)
4. Ask clarifying questions in board meeting

### FOR EXECUTIVE LEADERSHIP (EDA CEO, Director)
1. Read **VISION-2040-COMPREHENSIVE-RISK-ANALYSIS.md** (full document; 30 min skimming, 2 hours deep)
2. Distribute relevant sections to each team member (DC Director gets data center + financial risks, etc.)
3. Use **RISK-RESPONSE-PLAYBOOKS.md** for rapid decision-making when issues emerge
4. Conduct **monthly risk review** (update risk status in heat map; share with board)

### FOR STAFF (DC Director, FDI Officer, Housing Consultant, Communications Manager)
1. Read **relevant risk sections** (DC Director reads Sections 1, 2, 4; FDI Officer reads Sections 1, 2, 3, 5; etc.)
2. Understand **early warning signals** for your area of responsibility
3. Participate in **weekly standups** (report blockers; flag emerging risks)
4. Use **RISK-RESPONSE-PLAYBOOKS** if decision needed on the fly

### FOR CITY COMMISSION / COMMUNITY
1. Read **RISK-HEAT-MAP-EXECUTIVE-SUMMARY.md** (transparent overview of risks and mitigation)
2. Focus on **Worst Case Scenario section** (understand what could go wrong)
3. Understand **Quarterly Decision Gates** (Vision 2040 reviewed and adjusted quarterly)
4. Ask questions at board meetings; ensure transparency
