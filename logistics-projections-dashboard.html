Recruitment & Operations Strategy

  1. Years 1–3: Recruit 2–3 anchor tenants (200–350 jobs). Focus on 3PL, food processing.
  2. Years 3–7: Expand to 5–8 secondary facilities via targeted outreach to Midwest/South region.
  3. Years 7–15: Achieve 25–35% site utilization with mix of small/medium/niche manufacturers.
  4. Marketing: Leverage US-77 advantage, workforce development partnerships (USD 490), tax incentives.
  5. Workforce: Partner with community colleges, vocational programs to train 150–200 workers/year for new facilities.
