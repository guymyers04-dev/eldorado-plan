📋 How to Use This Tracker

Monitor Progress

Each project shows its current status, budget allocation, and key milestones. Check back regularly for updates.

Track the Budget

See how much has been allocated vs. spent on each project. This shows financial commitment and progress.

Understand Timelines

Projects are organized by phase (near-term, mid-term, long-term). Know when impacts will reach your area.

Find the Next Milestone

Each project shows its next key milestone and target date. Stay informed about upcoming announcements.

Identify Funding

See the mix of federal grants, state funding, city bonds, and private investment enabling each project.

Engage Intentionally

Contact your commissioner or the project owner to ask questions, offer support, or raise concerns.
