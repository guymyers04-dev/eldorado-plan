Accessory Dwelling Unit (ADU) Fast-Track Permitting

Housing
Quick Win
< $50K
< 6 months

Initiative Overview

What Is It?

Streamline ADU permitting to 14 days (vs. current 90+ days) by pre-approving architectural designs and reducing zoning variance requirements. This removes friction for homeowners who want to add rental income or house extended family.

Why El Dorado?

El Dorado has 1970s zoning rules; ADUs are cumbersome. 30% of homeowners express interest in ADUs if permitting were simple. Fast-track permitting could generate 50+ ADUs in 3 years = 50 new rental units.

Implementation Plan

1
Design & Zoning Review
Create "ADU Toolkit" (pre-approved designs, sample contracts, financing guide). Review/update zoning code to streamline ADU approval. Reduce variance requirements. Designate ADU specialist (city planner, 4 hrs/week). Months: 1–2.
2
Launch & Market
Publish 14-day permit target. Market via senior centers, neighborhoods, churches. Offer free design consultation. Process first 5 permits to test workflow. Months: 2–4.
3
Scale & Refine
Scale to 12 permits by Year 1. Gather homeowner feedback. Refine toolkit based on real-world experience. Target: 50+ ADUs by Year 3.
