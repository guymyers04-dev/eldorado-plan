Wichita Supply Chain Integration

Critical Strategy: "Secondary Supply Hub for Wichita"

Partner with Wichita Chamber to position El Dorado as a secondary Tier-2/Tier-3 hub. Many Wichita suppliers are capacity-constrained; El Dorado can absorb overflow work, provide backup manufacturing, and expand supply chain redundancy. This positioning is much stronger than competing on "generic manufacturing."

Recruitment Execution

Month 1–2: Wichita Supply Chain Map

  • Contact Wichita Chamber (Tom Busch, VP)
  • List top 50 aerospace suppliers
  • Identify multi-state operators
  • Research Bombardier U.S. supplier network

Month 2–4: Targeted Outreach

  • Direct mail + email to VP Operations (15 suppliers)
  • Host supply chain networking lunch in Wichita
  • Schedule facility tours
  • Present AS9100 certification readiness
