Why US-77 Beats I-35 for Logistics

  • Lower congestion: I-35 handles 80K+ vehicles/day; US-77 handles 22K—resulting in 75% faster truck transit times
  • Cost advantage: Lower industrial land costs ($24–30K/acre vs. $35–45K on I-35 corridors)
  • Direct market access: Straight shot to Wichita (36 mi), Oklahoma City (130 mi), Kansas City (210 mi)
  • Infrastructure ready: 4-lane highway, modern bridges, sewer/water/power capacity

Regional Integration Strategy

Position El Dorado as the "regional logistics hub" for the 4-state region (KS, OK, MO, AR) by leveraging the US-77 advantage for:

  • Distribution center recruitment: Target 50–100-person facilities (food, e-commerce, industrial)
  • Cross-docking operations: Lower-cost regional break bulk for I-35 and midwest markets
  • Workforce retention: 18,500 available workers in 30-mile radius; wages 15–20% lower than competitors
