Initiative Overview

What Is It?

A full-service marina complex featuring 150 slips, fuel dock, pump-out station, electric boat charging, ship's store, and water sports rentals. Located on North Gateway District waterfront, the marina creates seamless connection between new neighborhood, recreational activities, and visitor economy.

Why It Matters

El Dorado Lake draws 1M+ annual visitors but has zero commercial waterfront amenities. The west marina captures boater spending, anchors the waterfront dining/retail ecosystem, and serves as the primary water recreation hub for the region.

Project Specifications

Facility Components

  • Marina Slips: 150 (mixed sizes, 25-40 ft average)
  • Dry Stack Storage: 80 boats seasonal
  • Fuel Dock: Gasoline + diesel, 24/7 card access
  • Pump-Out Station: EPA-compliant waste management
  • Electric Charging: First in western Kansas
  • Ship's Store: 2,500 sf retail (navigation, safety, apparel)
  • Water Sports: Kayak/SUP rentals, sailboat rentals
  • Waterfront Plaza: 1-acre public event/gathering space
