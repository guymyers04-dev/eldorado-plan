Golf + Resort Development: Positioning El Dorado as Destination

Vision 2040 includes expansion of El Dorado's existing golf infrastructure into a multi-component golf + resort destination. This includes upgrading existing courses, developing a championship-caliber facility, and creating golf-community residential neighborhoods that attract regional golfers and tournament visitors.

New Construction

Championship Golf Course Development

A new 18-hole championship course (designed by nationally-known architect) becomes the centerpiece of El Dorado's golf destination strategy. Located on 160-180 acres, the course attracts regional tournaments, destination golfers, and supports resort operations.

Championship Course Specifications

Course Design

  • Yardage: 7,100+ yards (Championship tees)
  • Par: 72
  • Style: Links-inspired with native prairie
  • Handicap Range: 8.5-14.2
  • Course Rating: 74.5+
  • Tournament Capacity: PGA regional events, US Amateur qualifying

Supporting Facilities

  • Clubhouse: 25,000 sf (pro shop, restaurant, locker rooms)
  • Range Facility: 40-station range with covered bays
  • Practice Greens: Chipping area + short-game facility
  • Maintenance Facility: Equipment storage + workshop
  • Spectator Areas: Tournament viewing platforms

Residential Integration

Golf Community Residential Development

Golf course development anchors a 200-300 unit golf community with a mix of estate lots, townhomes, and apartments. This creates a mixed-income, golf-themed neighborhood that attracts golfers and residents seeking an active lifestyle.

Community Amenities
