## SUMMARY RANKINGS

### By Overall Competitive Strength
1. **Salina** — Largest (47K), strongest education (K-State), most resources
2. **El Dorado** — Unique advantages (water, I-35, state park), smallest but fastest-growing trajectory
3. **Dodge City** — Largest recent FDI win (Hilmar), rapid growth, food/ag focus
4. **Emporia** — Animal health specialty, university partnership, stable but less growth
5. **McPherson** — Established manufacturing, but stagnant; local threat to El Dorado on workforce
6. **Ponca City** — Declining; energy sector contraction; not a threat

### By Sector Competitiveness
- **Data Centers:** El Dorado (water) > Salina (size)
- **Aerospace Supply:** Salina (K-State) > El Dorado (I-35 logistics)
- **Advanced Manufacturing:** El Dorado (AMAZONE proof, I-35) > McPherson (local threat)
- **Agricultural Equipment:** El Dorado (AMAZONE proof, FTZ) > Emporia
- **Lake Tourism:** El Dorado (unique asset) >> all others
- **Food/Ag Processing:** Dodge City (Hilmar) >> all others (not El Dorado focus)
