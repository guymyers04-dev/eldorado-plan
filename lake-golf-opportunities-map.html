Regional Tournament Strategy

Position El Dorado as a destination for regional golf tournaments, sailing regattas, and water sports championships:

  • Golf: Host 4–6 regional amateur and corporate tournaments annually (Tue–Thu off-peak days)
  • Sailing: Partner with US Sailing to host Midwest District championships (July–August)
  • Fishing: Sponsor regional bass/walleye tournaments; promote via tournament circuit
  • Revenue synergy: Golf tournament attendees (avg 120/event) spend $2,400 on accommodations + meals per tournament
Sources: El Dorado Lake Master Plan (2024), ESRI Tourism Analytics, PGA Tour Regional Data, US Sailing, CoStar Recreation Benchmarks
