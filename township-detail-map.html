El Dorado Township: Detailed Local Planning & Development

Township Geography & Organization

El Dorado Township spans parts of 25S-04E, 25S-05E, 25S-06E, and 26S-05E in the Kansas Public Land Survey system (T-R coordinate system). The city proper occupies the central sections, with surrounding agricultural and rural land comprising the broader township area.

  • Primary Township: 25S-05E (El Dorado city center)
  • Adjacent Sections: 25S-04E (west), 25S-06E (east), 26S-05E (south)
  • City Grid: Regular street grid (1/8 mile blocks downtown; larger blocks periphery)
  • Area Covered: Approximately 30 square miles (township + immediate periphery)
  • Nearby Community: Prospect Township (25S-05E north)

📊 Development Inventory Summary

  • City Population: 12,694 (2024 est.) in ~4 sq mi urban core
  • Urban Density: ~3,200 people/sq mi (moderate)
  • Available Parcels (0.5–5 acres): ~120 commercial/industrial
  • Available Parcels (5–50 acres): ~18 North Gateway/South Industrial
  • Zoning Diversity: 7 zone types (R-1 through I-2 + AG)
  • Primary Growth Zones: North Gateway, Downtown TIF, South Industrial

Zoning Districts at a Glance

🏠 Residential Zones

  • R-1 Single-Family: Traditional neighborhoods; 5,000+ sq ft lots; low density
  • R-2/R-3 Multi-Family: Duplexes, apartments, mixed housing; moderate density
  • Developable Land: North Gateway zone (150–200 units planned)

💼 Commercial Zones

  • C-1 Highway Commercial: US-54/77 corridor; hotels, shops, services
  • C-2 Downtown Commercial: Central Ave; pedestrian-oriented; mixed-use
  • Downtown TIF District: Tax increment financing for revitalization

🏭 Industrial Zones

  • I-1 Light Industrial: Manufacturing, assembly, light warehousing
  • I-2 Heavy Industrial: Hazmat, heavy manufacturing, airport corridor
  • South Industrial Park: Project Horizon data center, aerospace campus

🌾 Agricultural Zone

  • AG Agricultural: Farms, ranches, ag-related equipment storage
  • Transition Zone: Gradual development at township edges

Key Development Opportunities

🏘️ North Gateway Development Zone

Location: US-54/77 north of city; 500+ acres potential

Zoning: Planned R-1, R-2/R-3, C-1 with mixed-use center

Opportunity: 150–200 housing units (workforce + market-rate); hotel; commercial services

Infrastructure: Water + sewer to north side; new roads; transit connection

📍 Downtown Revitalization (TIF District)

Location: Central Ave corridor (downtown core)

Zoning: C-2 Downtown Commercial; overlay for mixed-use development

Opportunity: 60–80 residential lofts; ground-floor retail; office

Incentive: Tax Increment Financing (TIF) district capturing 20-year sales tax growth

🏭 South Industrial & Aerospace Campus

Location: South Industrial corridor (US-54 + EQA Airport)

Zoning: I-1, I-2 Heavy Industrial; airport compatibility

Opportunity: Project Horizon data center campus; aerospace parts manufacturing; logistics warehousing

Infrastructure: Dedicated water supply; electrical capacity; broadband

💧 Lakeside Recreation District

Location: El Dorado Lake shoreline

Zoning: Recreation; mixed lodging + amenities

Opportunity: Hotel (100–150 rooms); RV park; boat rentals; dining/retail

Infrastructure: Marina expansion; parking; water/sewer capacity
