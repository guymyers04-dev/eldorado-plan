How to Use

The Phase 11 tools are designed to support three key planning functions:

  • Scenario Simulator: For city planners and council members to evaluate different development strategies and their long-term impacts
  • Labor Forecast: For residents, educators, and workforce development professionals to understand in-demand jobs and training opportunities
  • Opportunity Scanner: For investors, developers, and entrepreneurs to identify high-potential projects with strong ROI and market alignment
