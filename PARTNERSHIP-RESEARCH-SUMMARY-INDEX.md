- 1st follow-up (Day 3): Check-in email
- 2nd follow-up (Day 7): Phone voicemail

- Persistence framework (expected success rates: 10–15% response, 5–10% discovery call, 2–5% site visit, <1% LOI)

**Part 4: Common Objections & Response Scripts**
- "We're only looking at [Texas/California]" → Why El Dorado is different
- "Our timeline is too tight (6 months)" → Honesty + future pipeline positioning
- "We need [specific requirement]" → Differentiation + alternative positioning
- "We've never heard of El Dorado" → AMAZONE precedent + credibility building
- "We need to see [market data]" → Proactive data sharing + follow-up

**Part 5: Negotiation Framework**
- Key LOI components (project description, land, incentives, performance conditions, timeline)
- El Dorado's red lines (job creation, local hiring, community benefit)
- Negotiable items (land price, tax abatement length, permitting)
- Common requests (10-year abatement, below-market land, workforce help, utility guarantees)

**Final Checklist: 90-Day Success Metrics**
- Outreach completed (80–100+ contacts, 40–50+ emails, 20–30+ calls)
- Active conversations (30–40 prospects, 20+ scheduled calls, 8–12 site visits)
- Materials developed (contact database, customized packages, market studies, case studies)
- Relationship milestones (1st site visit, feedback loop, champion identified)
- Grant/capital prep (EDA Good Jobs Challenge, EB-5 pre-qual, municipal bonds, LIHTC)
