## KEY SUCCESS FACTORS

1. **Start small, show wins fast** — Phase 1 delivers 7 major additions in 8 weeks. Visible progress builds political support.

2. **User research is critical** — Don't build dashboard without talking to site selectors, job seekers, or residents. Test assumptions.

3. **Interactivity > walls of text** — Dashboard, calculator, quest system, pledge wall all beat documentation. People don't read 50-page docs.

4. **Community co-creation** — Ambassador program, idea incubator, and participatory budgeting turn residents into co-creators. This builds ownership.

5. **Metrics matter** — Track engagement (users, comments, pledges, attendance). Report monthly. Adjust based on data.
