Role Tiers Explained

🏛️ Decision Authority

Mayor, City Manager, Commissioners, Department Heads — make formal decisions on Vision 2040 projects.

🚀 Enabler

Economic development leaders, business organizations, and chambers — create enabling conditions for projects.

⚙️ Implementer

School districts, colleges, healthcare — carry out Vision 2040 initiatives through their operations.

🌍 Force Multiplier

County, regional partners — amplify El Dorado's efforts at county and regional levels.

💬 Advocate

Nonprofit leaders, business owners, community members — advocate for and support Vision 2040 projects.
