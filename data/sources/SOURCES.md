## Primary Data Sources

### Federal & State Data

DataUSA. (n.d.). *El Dorado, KS profile*. Retrieved June 28, 2026, from https://datausa.io/profile/geo/el-dorado-ks/

**Summary:** Primary source for demographic data, economic indicators, employment statistics, and population trends for El Dorado, Kansas. Includes real-time Census data, wage information, and educational attainment.

**Data Points Included:**
- Population demographics
- Median household income
- Employment by industry
- Educational attainment
- Housing characteristics
- Wage & salary data

### Health & Demographics

Kansas Health Matters. (2026). *Demographics dashboard*. Kansas Health Institute. Retrieved June 28, 2026, from https://www.kansashealthmatters.org/indicators/index/dashboard?alias=demographics

**Summary:** Kansas state health indicators and demographic data specific to Butler County and El Dorado region. Provides health disparities, population segments, and socioeconomic indicators.

**Data Points Included:**
- Population by age and gender
- Race/ethnicity demographics
- Poverty rates
- Housing and living conditions
- Access to amenities

---

Kansas Health Matters. (2026). *Detailed indicator cards — Locales: Butler County*. Kansas Health Institute. Retrieved June 28, 2026, from https://www.kansashealthmatters.org/indicators/index/dashboard?module=indicators&controller=index&action=dashboard&id=131872279519160803&card=0&localeId=167981

**Summary:** Granular indicator dashboard with filterable metrics by locale (Butler County and surrounding regions). Includes quality-of-life indicators, environmental factors, and community health measures.

**Data Points Included:**

- Time-series trend data
