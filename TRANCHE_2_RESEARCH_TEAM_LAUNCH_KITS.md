# RESEARCH TEAM 2: Developer Feasibility

## Mission
Contact LIHTC (Low-Income Housing Tax Credit) developers and multifamily operators. Determine housing pipeline for Vision 2040 (124 Lofts pilot + 200+ units by 2032). Secure preliminary interest + financing sources.

## Owner
Finance Director

## Week 1 Tasks (6 tasks, ~10 hours)

- [ ] **Task 2.1** (2 hrs): Research LIHTC program rules + Kansas opportunities
  - LIHTC basics: 9% vs. 4% credits, annual allocation, competition, timeline
  - Kansas-specific: KDFA contact, recent LIHTC projects, typical mix (affordable + market-rate)
  - Output: 1-page LIHTC summary + 3-4 potential Kansas developers who've done similar projects

- [ ] **Task 2.2** (1.5 hrs): Identify 5 LIHTC developers to approach
  - Criteria: Track record in Kansas/Midwest, mixed-income experience, $5M–$30M projects, mission-aligned
  - Research targets: Search KDFA awards database, NAHC directory, CoStar
  - Output: Developer list + contact info (CEO/development director)

- [ ] **Task 2.3** (2 hrs): Compile pilot project case studies (124 Lofts comparable)
  - Find 3–4 LIHTC mixed-use projects in Midwest small cities (similar to El Dorado)
  - Case study format: Project size, affordability %,  financing mix (LIHTC %, city incentives %, private), timeline, outcomes
  - Output: 1-page comparison chart + project profiles (ready for developer conversations)

- [ ] **Task 2.4** (2 hrs): Draft housing finance roadmap framework
  - Capital structure: LIHTC (40–50%), CDBG (10–15%), city incentives (5–10%), private equity (25–35%)
  - Timeline: LOI (month 1), financing closed (month 6–8), construction start (month 9)
  - Milestones: Land acquisition, city approvals, financing commitments, groundbreaking
  - Output: 2-page housing finance framework (ready for developer interviews)

- [ ] **Task 2.5** (1.5 hrs): Research 124 Lofts site + land assembly strategy
  - Current ownership, zoning, infrastructure needs, environmental clearance status
  - City incentive capacity: Abatement, TIF, land donation, infrastructure cost-sharing
  - Output: Site brief + incentive options sheet (to present to developers)

- [ ] **Task 2.6** (1 hr): Schedule 3–5 developer discovery calls (Jul 8–15)
  - Goal: Determine initial interest in 124 Lofts pilot + larger Vision 2040 pipeline
  - Format: 45-min phone calls (not formal proposal yet)
  - Interviewer: Finance Director (optionally with consultant)

## Stakeholder Interviews (Jul 5–15)

**Primary Interview: Finance Director + City Engineer (permitting overview)**
- Duration: 90 min (combined)
- Goal: Understand city's permitting + incentive authority before approaching developers
- Schedule: Jul 5–6
- Interviewer: City Manager (leading Finance discussion) + City Engineer (leading permitting discussion)
- Output: Housing finance roadmap + permitting timeline (internal use)

**Secondary Interviews: 3–5 developer discovery calls (Jul 8–15)**
