💡 Tips for Making an Impact

  • Be specific: Reference the exact Vision 2040 project you're supporting or questioning
  • Be personal: Share how a project affects you, your family, or your neighborhood
  • Be respectful: Address commissioners professionally, even if you disagree
  • Be brief: Stick to 2 minutes (about 250 words) — commissioners appreciate conciseness
  • Come prepared: Bring 5-10 copies of your comment to distribute to commissioners
  • Follow up: Email commissioners after the meeting if you want to expand on your comments
