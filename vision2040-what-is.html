What is Vision 2040?

El Dorado's Platform for Shared Economic Growth

Vision 2040 Explained

Vision 2040 is a digital platform connecting business leaders, investors, young professionals, students, and community members around shared goals of economic growth, opportunity, and community belonging.

It's built BY El Dorado, FOR El Dorado. The platform unites the city's existing assets—1,559 Chamber member businesses, 2,000+ students, 160+ nonprofit organizations—into one coordinated community leadership ecosystem.

The Problem We're Solving

El Dorado Has Momentum. But We're Fragmented.

  • 1,559 Chamber businesses working in silos, limited coordination
  • 2,000+ students with no visibility into opportunities in their hometown
  • 160+ nonprofit organizations with overlapping missions, limited collaboration
  • Young talent leaving because they don't see opportunity in El Dorado
  • Business owners burning out serving on too many committees

Our Solution

One Unified Platform. Connecting Everyone.

Vision 2040 is where all these groups meet, collaborate, and accelerate growth together.

Instead of fragmented efforts, Vision 2040 creates:

  • Market Intelligence: Real-time data on jobs, properties, investment opportunities
  • Investor Access: Deal flow, ROI calculators, capital connections
  • Talent Pipeline: Direct connection between employers and 2,000+ students
  • Network Amplification: One message reaching all 1,559 Chamber members
  • Community Engagement: Transparent communication and shared decision-making

Four Key Benefits

📊

Market Intelligence

Real-time data on job forecasts, property development pipeline, market trends, and competitive benchmarking.

💼

Investor Access

Deal flow dashboard with 12+ active projects, ROI calculators, and direct connections to capital sources.

👥

Talent Pipeline

Direct connection to 2,000+ USD 490 students, skills assessments, and apprenticeship programs.

🤝

Network Amplification

Co-branded marketing reaching 1,559+ Chamber members. Collaboration opportunities with complementary businesses.
