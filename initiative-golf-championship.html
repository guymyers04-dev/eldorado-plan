Championship Golf Course Development

Place & Recreation
Phase 2-3
$12-16M
24 months

Initiative Overview

What Is It?

Design and construct an 18-hole championship golf course on 160-180 acres. Links-inspired design by nationally-recognized architect. Targets PGA regional events, U.S. Amateur qualifying, destination tournaments.

Why It Matters

Championship course elevates El Dorado to regional golf destination status. Attracts tournament visitors (multi-day stays = high visitor spending), supports resort/hotel development, and anchors quality-of-life positioning for affluent relocations.

Course Specifications

Championship Specifications

  • Layout: 18 holes, par 72
  • Yardage: 7,100-7,500 yards (championship tees)
  • Course Rating: 74.5+
  • Slope Rating: 140+
  • Design Style: Links-inspired with Kansas prairie character
  • Handicap Capacity: 8.5-14.2 (all skill levels)
  • Tournament Ready: PGA regional events, U.S. Amateur qualifying

Supporting Facilities

  • Clubhouse: 25,000 sf (pro shop, restaurant, locker rooms)
  • Driving Range: 40-station range (covered + open)
  • Practice Facility: Full short-game area (chipping, pitching)
  • Maintenance Facility: 8,000 sf equipment storage
  • Spectator Infrastructure: Seating areas for tournaments
  • Cart Storage: 150-vehicle capacity

Financial Model

Capital Investment

  • Land Acquisition: $1.5-2M
  • Course Design & Permitting: $500K
  • Course Construction: $7-8M
  • Clubhouse + Facilities: $3-4M
  • Equipment + Furnishings: $1.5-2M
  • Total Capital: $13.5-16.5M

Annual Operating Revenue (Year 5 Mature)

  • Green Fees: $1.4-1.7M (28K-32K rounds @ $45-55)
  • Cart Rentals: $300-400K
  • Food & Beverage: $400-500K (clubhouse restaurant)
  • Tournament/Event Fees: $150-200K
  • Merchandise & Retail: $150-200K
  • Total Annual Revenue: $2.4-3.0M

Tournament & Events Program

Annual Tournament Schedule

PGA Regional Events

2-3 events/year (qualifying rounds, sectionals). 200-400 participants each.

U.S. Amateur Qualifying

Annual qualifying round (150+ players). High profile USGA event.

State Amateur Championships

Kansas Golf Association events. 100-150 participants, multi-day.

Corporate Outings

250-350 events/year from regional corporations. Average 80-100 participants each.

Destination Packages

Golf packages (3-5 days) with resort + course bundled. High-value tourism.

Club Tournaments

Member tournaments + memberships available. Equity or public daily fee.

Visitor Economy Impact

Championship course attracts destination golfers, generating significant regional tourism:

Tournament Visitor Spending

  • Annual Tournament Visitors: 1,500-2,000
  • Avg. Length of Stay: 2-4 days
  • Avg. Spending/Visitor: $500-800
  • Total Tournament Spending: $750K-1.6M/year

Destination Package Golfers

  • Annual Destination Package Groups: 20-30
  • Avg. Group Size: 12-16 golfers
  • Avg. Group Spending: $12-16K (resort + golf bundle)
  • Total Destination Spending: $240-480K/year
Annual Visitor Spending Generated by Course $1.0-2.1M
