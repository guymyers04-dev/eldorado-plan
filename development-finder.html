Market Context & Comparables

📊 El Dorado Market Overview

  • Available Commercial/Industrial Land: ~120–150 acres across all zones
  • Land Cost (Avg): $12,000–18,000/acre (industrial); $25,000–35,000/acre (commercial)
  • Median Commercial Rent: $8–12/sq ft/year
  • Residential Market: Median $153K (strong growth opportunity)
  • Job Growth (Target): +150–200 net new jobs/year through 2040
  • Tax Incentives: 10–15 year tax abatement; TIF financing available

🏘️ Comparable Regional Markets

  • Andover, KS (Wichita suburb): $18–25K/acre commercial; rapid residential growth
  • Augusta, KS (Regional hub): $10–15K/acre industrial; established logistics cluster
  • Newton, KS (AGCO headquarters): $15–22K/acre industrial; manufacturing corridor
  • Emporia, KS (University town): $12–18K/acre mixed-use; education-anchored growth

El Dorado Advantage: 20–30% lower land costs than Wichita suburbs; superior highway access (I-35 + US-54/77); established utilities & water supply.

ROI Calculator & Investment Models

Click "Calculate ROI" on any property card to estimate your investment returns based on:

  • Acquisition Cost: Land + development fees

  • Revenue Model: Rent, occupancy assumptions, or sales price
  • Operating Costs: Utilities, maintenance, property tax

📈 Sample Investment Scenarios

  • Scenario 1 (Industrial/Logistics): 8-acre site + $800K investment = $2.2M revenue (5-yr), 35% ROI
  • Scenario 2 (Residential): 15-acre site + $3M investment = $1.8M annual rent (80-unit), 32% ROI
  • Scenario 3 (Mixed-Use): 5-acre downtown TIF site + $2.5M = $2.8M blended revenue, 28% ROI

All scenarios assume 70–80% occupancy, market-rate pricing, and standard financing. Use the ROI calculator for property-specific analysis.
