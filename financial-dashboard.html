Data & Methodology

Vision 2040 Financial Projections (Phase 8)

  • Investment figures: Engineering estimates, comparable project costs, regional precedents
  • Job projections: Bureau of Labor Statistics occupational data, operational ratios (jobs per $M invested)
  • Tax revenue: El Dorado municipal rate structures, property tax assessments, sales tax assumptions
  • Economic multiplier: Standard regional economic impact models (1.5–1.6x for mixed development portfolios)

Butler County Fiscal Data (2025)

  • Source: Butler County Audited Financial Statements (Year Ended December 31, 2025)
  • Total Funds: $55,035,379 (ending unencumbered cash balance)
  • Annual Receipts: $76,043,727 (FY 2025)
  • Special Purpose Funds: $45,496,440 (largest reserve pool for infrastructure investment)
  • Special Highway Improvement Fund: $8,930,135 (direct corridor investment capacity)
  • Audit Firm: Loyd Group, LLC, Galva, Kansas

County Coordination Intent

Butler County's fiscal health and substantial reserves demonstrate capacity to co-invest in Vision 2040 priorities. County participation in economic development initiatives, joint infrastructure agreements, and JEDA (Joint Economic Development Agreement) coordination are critical for optimizing state and federal grant access and accelerating implementation timelines.
