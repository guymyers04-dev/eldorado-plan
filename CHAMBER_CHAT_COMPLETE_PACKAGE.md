## 💡 TIPS FOR SUCCESS

### PRESENTATION
- **Practice out loud** 2–3 times before July 1
- **Make eye contact** with 3 different people during the pitch
- **Pause** after key statistics (let them sink in)
- **Smile** — you believe in this. Show it.
- **Have water** nearby. Sip between slides.

### MATERIALS
- **Print extras** (always have more 1-pagers than attendees)
- **Use color** for 1-pagers (stands out, professional)
- **QR codes work** — test them before the event
- **Pens** — keep 10+ on hand (people steal them)

### FOLLOW-UP
- **Call hot leads same day** (48-hour window is huge)

- **Build ambassador relationships** (they're your super volunteers)
- **Track everything** (data becomes your August strategy)
