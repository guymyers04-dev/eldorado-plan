### **community-engagement.html** (30 KB, audience-focused)
**Purpose:** Build public buy-in and persuade stakeholders to support Vision 2040.

**Contents:**

- **Core Messaging Framework (4 levels)**
  - 30-second elevator pitch
  - 2-3 minute fact summary
  - Emotional "heart" message
  - Proof point (AMAZONE 2025 win)

- **Audience-Specific Talking Points** (6 audiences)
  - Educators (USD 490, USD 375, Butler CC): Education as economic engine
  - Healthcare leaders (SBA Hospital): Access problem solved, population growth, $80M projects, NHSC recruitment
  - Business community: Revenue growth, workforce expansion, development opportunities, tax base
  - Residents: Safety improvements, lake activation, downtown vitality, schools, property values
  - Investors/lenders: Federal backing, FDI proof, strong tax base, EB-5 visa program, STAR Bonds
  - Skeptics: Historical precedent, AMAZONE proof, federal backing, transparency, reversibility

- **8 Common Objections & Comprehensive Responses**
  1. "Too ambitious" → Phased approach, proven assets, quarterly transparency
  2. "Where's the money?" → Federal/state grants (30-40%), private investment (60-65%), debt management
  3. "Lose small-town character" → Growth without losing identity, healthy vitality, prevents decline
  4. "Mega-site won't get tenants" → AMAZONE proof of concept, foundational work de-risks, fallback plans
  5. "Why believe this will happen?" → Quarterly scorecards, leadership accountability, multi-stakeholder commitment
  6. "Environmental impact?" → EPA smart growth programs, habitat protection, watershed coordination
  7. "Benefits businesses more than people" → Affordability programs (LIHTC, RHID, MHC expansion), local hiring preferences
  8. Plus crisis responses (grant rejection, deal stalls, negative media, slower growth)

- **Communication Tactics by Phase**
  - Launch phase (Jan–Mar 2026): Press, town halls, website, media coverage
  - Education phase (Apr–Dec 2026): Monthly town halls, stakeholder briefings, social media, email, video, community board
  - Early wins phase (2027–2028): Quarterly scorecards, grant celebrations, groundbreakings, spotlights
  - Momentum phase (2029–2034): Annual summit, success stories, peer comparisons, media partnerships

- **Why 8 Different Stakeholder Groups Support Vision 2040**
  - Business owners: Customers, markets, tax base
  - Families with children: Schools, childcare, jobs, recreation
  - Healthcare workers: SBA expansion, career growth, shortage = security
  - Educators: Student growth, facilities, compensation, workforce partnerships
  - Young adults: Local jobs, affordable housing, culture, education pathways
  - Seniors: Senior care, healthcare, recreation, property values
  - Homeowners: 3-5% annual appreciation, infrastructure investment, community safety
  - Construction trades: 15-year building boom, higher wages

- **Crisis Communication Playbook**
  - If grant rejected: Portfolio approach, alternative funding, progress on other fronts
  - If mega-site stalls: FDI long-term play, AMAZONE validates strategy, other projects provide growth
  - If negative media: Data transparency, acknowledge concerns, success stories, community engagement
  - If population growth slower: Long-term timeline, quality over quantity, employment growth, peer comparisons

- **Communication Effectiveness Metrics**
  - Reach: Email subscribers (3K+), social followers (2K+), town hall attendance (100+), website visits (5K+/mo)
  - Sentiment: Favorable opinion (70%+), understanding (70%+), support growth (65%+)
  - Action: Commission votes (90%+), business support (100+), volunteers (50+), press mentions (20+/yr)
