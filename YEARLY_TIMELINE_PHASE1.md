## CRITICAL DECISION GATES

### Decision Gate 1: Q4 2027 (Data Center Market Entry)

**Decision:** Does El Dorado secure 1+ data center LOI or strong Stage 3 discussions?

**If YES (GO):**
- Accelerate data center infrastructure + site prep
- Phase 2 confirms data center as anchor tenant
- Scale other sectors in support

**If NO (NO-GO):**
- Pivot to FDI manufacturing as primary driver
- Accelerate hospitality + lake activation
- Extend data center prospecting timeline to 2028–2029
- Adjust financial projections downward (conservative case: 1.7x–2.3x multiplier)

**Contingency:** All scenarios remain positive (downside ROI 1.7x–2.3x). Phase continues regardless; timing/scope adjusts.

---

### Decision Gate 2: Q4 2028 (FDI + Housing Momentum)

**Decision:** Are 2–3 FDI LOIs signed AND 2+ housing projects advancing?

**If YES (GO):**
- Confirm Phase 1 trajectory on track
- Authorize Phase 2 expansion (additional FDI, housing, data centers)
- Increase recruiting/staffing investment

**If NO (NO-GO or CAUTION):**
- Assess macro conditions (recession, policy changes)
- Maintain current execution pace; don't over-commit
- Adjust Phase 2 expectations
- Consider alternative sectors (food processing, agtech)

**Contingency:** If underperforming, Phase 1 timeline extends to 2031 (vs. 2030 planned completion). Still achieves positive ROI.

---

### Decision Gate 3: Q4 2029 (Phase 1 Readiness for Phase 2)

**Decision:** Has Phase 1 achieved 80%+ of targets (population 8,500+, tax base $35M+, jobs 700+)?

**If YES (GO):**
- Authorize Phase 2 execution (2030–2034)
- Commit capital to data center #2, FDI facilities 4–5, additional housing
- Expand organizational capacity (team growth, new initiatives)
- Plan Phase 3 (2034–2040)

**If NO (CAUTION/REPLAN):**
- Reassess Phase 2 ambitions
- Focus on stabilizing Phase 1 projects
- Extend timeline to 2031–2032 for Phase 2 launch
- Review organizational effectiveness

**Contingency:** Even at 50% of Phase 1 targets, Phase 2 authorized but at reduced scale.
