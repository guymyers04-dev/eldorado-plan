Frequently Asked Questions

Can I do more than one of these? +

Absolutely! Many people are ambassadors AND sponsors. You can mix and match based on your interests and capacity. Choose what feels right for you.

What if I don't have much time? +

Ambassador is just 30 minutes per month, and the monthly training call is optional. You can also simply share content when it fits your schedule. No pressure—do what works for you.

Is there a cost to be an ambassador? +

No! Being an ambassador is completely free. We provide all materials, training, and support. The only cost is if you decide to sponsor an event.

Who can be an ambassador? +

Anyone who is passionate about El Dorado's future! Business owners, nonprofit leaders, educators, community advocates, young professionals, students—we're looking for all kinds of leaders.

What sponsorship options are available? +

Several! July 4 Independence Day Booth ($250-$500), Butler County Fair ($750-$1,500), and monthly First Fridays ($250+). Check the sponsorship page for full details and benefits at each level.

How do I sign up? +

Ambassadors: Fill out the form on the Ambassador page. Sponsors: Contact us via the sponsorship page. Partners: Send us a message using the form below or email info@eldorado.gov.
