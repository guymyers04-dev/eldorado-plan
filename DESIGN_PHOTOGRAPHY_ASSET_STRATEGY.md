## Part 2: Sourcing Strategies

### Option A: Local Photographer (Recommended)
**Best for authenticity and cost-effectiveness**

**Approach:**
1. Contact El Dorado Chamber of Commerce
2. Ask for photographer recommendations
3. Search local photography businesses
4. Get portfolio samples
5. Request day rate ($500-800/day) for 2-3 days
6. Plan shoots strategically (sunrise/sunset, weather)

**Advantages:**
- ✅ Authentic El Dorado imagery
- ✅ Local knowledge
- ✅ Flexible scheduling
- ✅ Good value ($800-1600 for 50+ images)
- ✅ Can include lifestyle/people shots
- ✅ Unlimited revisions

**Disadvantages:**
- ❌ Quality varies by photographer
- ❌ Longer turnaround
- ❌ May need direction/guidance

**Cost:** $1,000-1,800 for 50-80 images  
**Timeline:** 4-5 weeks

**Shoot List Template:**
```
Day 1: Landscapes & Architecture
├── El Dorado Lake sunrise
├── El Dorado Lake sunset
├── Downtown Main Street (multiple angles)
├── Central Avenue (storefronts)
├── City skyline (if possible)
├── Parks and recreation areas
├── El Dorado Lake marina/boats
└── Regional landscape (agricultural)

Day 2: Business & Industrial
├── ADM facilities (exterior)
├── Manufacturing/industrial areas
├── Downtown business district
├── Local businesses (restaurants, shops)
├── Agricultural landscape/equipment
├── Oil heritage sites (if applicable)
└── Construction/development sites

Day 3: Community & People (if photographer provides)
├── Public events/gatherings
├── School campus tours
├── Community leaders (with permission)
├── Families enjoying city amenities
├── Local business owners
└── Young professionals
```

### Option B: Premium Stock Photography
**For supplementary images, not primary content**

**Services:**
- Unsplash Premium ($96/year) — Free + premium images
- Shutterstock ($55-165/month) — Huge library
- Adobe Stock ($40-60/month) — Quality library
- Getty Images (expensive) — Premium quality

**Advantages:**
- ✅ Instant access
- ✅ High quality guaranteed
- ✅ No usage restrictions (usually)
- ✅ Fast implementation

**Disadvantages:**
- ❌ Generic (not authentic to El Dorado)
- ❌ May be used by competitors
- ❌ Less emotional impact
- ❌ Ongoing cost

**Cost:** $50-200/month  
**Best for:** Supplementary images, initial placeholder improvements

**Recommended usage:**
- Hero sections while waiting for commissioned shots
- Industry-specific imagery (aerospace, biotech, ag)
- Lifestyle imagery
- Atmospheric/seasonal imagery

### Option C: User-Contributed Content
**Tap El Dorado residents & business community**

**Strategy:**
1. Create photo submission form on website
2. Offer small incentive ($25-50 per published photo)
3. Provide usage rights agreement
4. Curate best submissions
5. Credit photographers on website

**Advantages:**
- ✅ Very authentic
- ✅ Community engagement
- ✅ Cost-effective
- ✅ Fresh perspectives

**Disadvantages:**
- ❌ Quality varies significantly
- ❌ Slow process
- ❌ Requires curation effort
- ❌ May need rights clarification

**Cost:** $500-1,500 for 30-40 quality images  
**Timeline:** 6-8 weeks

## Part 4: Image Specifications & Technical Requirements

### Responsive Image Sizes
**Create 3 sizes for each image:**

```
Size                Resolution      Use Case              File Size (WebP)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Small (mobile)      640×480         Mobile devices        60-80KB
Medium (tablet)     1280×960        Tablets, small desk   150-200KB
Large (desktop)     1920×1440       Desktop, hero section 300-400KB
X-Large (4K)        3840×2880       Future, print, retina 800-1000KB
```

### Naming Convention
```
Category-Description-Dimensions.Format

Examples:
- hero-eldorado-lake-1920x1080.webp
- landscape-agriculture-sunrise-1280x960.webp
- industry-aerospace-facility-1920x1440.webp
- people-mayor-headshot-640x480.webp
- community-downtown-main-street-1920x1440.webp
```

### Compression Guidelines
```
Image Type          Format      Quality     Target Size
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Photography        WebP        80%         200-400KB
Photographs        JPEG        85%         250-450KB
Complex scenes     PNG         N/A         300-500KB
Lifestyle/People   WebP        80%         150-300KB
Icons/Graphics     SVG         N/A         5-20KB
```

### Technical Metadata
Include in all images:
- Title (for sharing)
- Description (for SEO)
- Keywords (indexed images)
- Copyright/Credit
- Location (GPS if applicable)
- Shoot date
- Photographer name

**Add to image EXIF via tool:**
```bash
# Using ExifTool
exiftool -Title="El Dorado Lake Sunrise" \
         -Description="Scenic sunrise over El Dorado Lake in Kansas" \
         -Copyright="© 2026 El Dorado, Kansas" \
         -Creator="John Smith Photography" \
         image.jpg
```

```
Photographer: [Name]

Project: El Dorado Vision 2040 Website
Date: [Date]

1. GRANT OF RIGHTS
   Photographer grants Client a perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free 
   license to use the photographs for:
   - Website publication
   - Marketing materials
   - Social media
   - Print materials
   - Presentations

2. EXCLUSIONS
   Photographer retains right to:
   - Display in portfolio
   - Use for award submissions
   - License to non-competing entities

3. CREDIT
   Client agrees to provide photographer credit on website
   (e.g., "Photography by John Smith" or watermark)

4. PAYMENT
   Client pays $[amount] for [number of days] of photography
   and unlimited use of resulting images.

5. DELIVERY
   Photographer delivers images in:
   - Original RAW files
   - High-resolution JPEGs
   - Edited versions
   - WebP versions (small, medium, large)
```

### Stock Photo Licensing
Ensure all stock images have:
- ✅ Commercial use license
- ✅ No attribution required (optional but nice)
- ✅ Unlimited web usage
- ✅ No model release conflicts
