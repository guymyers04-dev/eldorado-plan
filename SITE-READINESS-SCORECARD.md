## CONTINGENCY PLANNING

### Scenario A: Delayed Data Center Decision (Risk Level: MEDIUM)

**Trigger:** No data center LOI by end Q4 2027

**Contingency Actions:**
- Accelerate 2nd/3rd FDI recruitment (agricultural equipment)
- Increase industrial manufacturing site marketing
- Expand logistics center recruitment (Amazon, XPO, etc.)
- Redirect power/water infrastructure investment to Phase 2 housing + healthcare

**ROI Impact:** $100–200M lower first decade, but 1,600-job target still achievable via diversified recruitment

---

### Scenario B: Federal/State Funding Cuts (Risk Level: MEDIUM)

**Trigger:** EDA, USDA, or HUD grant reductions >20%

**Contingency Actions:**
- Increase municipal bond issuance (up to $30–40M GO bond capacity)
- Expand private development partnerships (co-development with private REITs)
- Accelerate user fee / rate increases for utilities (water, sewer, power)
- Seek additional philanthropic capital for downtown/lake projects

**ROI Impact:** Timeline extension (2–3 years), but project economics still viable

---

### Scenario C: Community Resistance to Growth (Risk Level: LOW)

**Trigger:** City commission votes down major project (e.g., zoning change, incentive package)

**Contingency Actions:**
- Expand community engagement / town halls
- Demonstrate fiscal benefits via updated pro formas
- Slow implementation timeline to allow community buy-in
- Emphasize local hiring + workforce development benefits

**ROI Impact:** Delayed execution, but projects ultimately proceed with community support

---

### Scenario D: Private Developer Withdrawal (Risk Level: MEDIUM)
