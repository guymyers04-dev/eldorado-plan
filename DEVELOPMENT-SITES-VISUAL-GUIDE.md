- Concession agreements required for glamping, dining, water sports operations
- Lake boundary: State park perimeter (navigable water boundary)
- Commercial use permits: Standard for restaurant, lodging, recreation

**Federal (USACE):**
- Jurisdiction: Army Corps of Engineers controls navigable waterway
- Permits: Section 404 only if dredging/fill in jurisdictional waters (not anticipated)
- Dam safety: El Dorado Lake dam engineered for 1,000-year flood event

**Environmental Permitting:**
- Wetlands: No jurisdictional wetlands identified in development zones
- Endangered species: No federally protected species in project area
- Water discharge (restaurant, bathroom facilities): Standard NPDES compliance

## SITE 6: UTILITY INFRASTRUCTURE ANCHORS

### Water Infrastructure

**Kate Donnelly Water Treatment Plant:**
- **Location:** City-operated (south El Dorado area)
- **Coordinates:** 37.7850°N, 97.4000°W (approximate)
- **Capacity:** 14 MGD (current throughput)
- **Source:** El Dorado Lake (city-owned water rights)
- **Excess Capacity:** 9–10 MGD available (as of 2026)
- **Treatment Process:** Conventional (settling, filtration, chlorination, fluoridation)
- **Water Quality:** Award-winning (potable standard, "Best Tasting")
- **Distribution Network:** 12–15 inch mains extend to downtown + north/west quadrants

**Water Main Extension (B.A.S.E. Grant Project):**
- **Route:** From treatment plant north to megasite (approximately 8 miles)
- **Pipe Size:** 16 inches (adequate for industrial flows)
- **Status:** 60% complete (as of June 2026)
- **Completion:** Q4 2026
- **Cost:** $2.5M (B.A.S.E. grant $2M, KDOT co-funding $500K)

**Industrial Water Supply Agreement (In Draft):**
- **Allocation:** 5–8 MGD reserved for megasite industrial use
- **Rate:** $0.85–$1.05 per 1,000 gallons (vs. residential $2.10)
- **Term:** 20–30 year contract (renewable)
- **Performance:** Guaranteed supply, minimum 23 MGD (contract with USACE through 2040)
- **Seasonal:** No constraints (lake + groundwater backup ensure year-round supply)

**Evergy (Formerly Westar) Substation:**
- **Name:** East El Dorado Substation
