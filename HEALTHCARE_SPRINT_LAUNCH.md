# Healthcare Economy Sprint — Full Launch
**Start Date:** July 1, 2026 | **Duration:** 12-week acceleration sprint  
**Target:** Phase 1A groundbreaking (Q4 2025) → First impact (Q3 2026)

---

## 🎯 Sprint Mission

Launch El Dorado's $95M healthcare economy transformation through coordinated action across 4 workstreams:
1. **Funding** - Secure $50-60M in federal grants + $35-40M local capital
2. **Facilities** - Design & permit surgical center + behavioral health wing
3. **Workforce** - Launch K-12 pathway + Butler CC programs + physician recruitment
4. **Partnerships** - Align SBA Hospital, USD 490, Butler CC, city government
