## CONTINGENCY PLANS

**If City Commission Delays Vote:**
- [ ] Request special City Commission meeting (don't wait for regular meeting)
- [ ] Offer to brief individual commissioners if objections exist
- [ ] Frame as time-sensitive (market window urgency)
- [ ] Consider media pressure (publish plan publicly if not confidential)

**If EDA Board is Unsupportive:**
- [ ] Meet with Board Chair individually (understand objections)
- [ ] Identify swing voters and address their specific concerns
- [ ] Consider presenting business case study from peer city
- [ ] If board rejects, consider taking directly to City Commission

**If Chamber Attendance is Low:**
- [ ] Schedule additional member breakfast/briefing
- [ ] Offer one-on-one briefings for high-profile members
- [ ] Use Chamber board members as ambassadors
- [ ] Target member segments (construction, retail, services) with direct outreach

**If Community Townhall has Organized Opposition:**
- [ ] Address objections transparently in Q&A
- [ ] Offer to schedule follow-up meeting with opposition group
- [ ] Provide detailed FAQ or written response to specific concerns
- [ ] Don't be defensive; listen and incorporate feedback

**If Brokers/Agencies Decline Engagement:**
- [ ] Try alternative brokers/agencies (backup contacts identified)
- [ ] Hire independent consultant if necessary
- [ ] Direct outreach to operators without broker (higher effort, doable)
