## PART 8: TROUBLESHOOTING

### Issue: Email bounced

**Diagnosis:**
```
□ Check: Is email address correct?
□ Try: Check company website for correct format
□ Check: LinkedIn for alternative
□ Try: info@[company].com as fallback
□ Try: Switch to other contact or higher-up (CFO/CEO)
```

**Action:**
```
Update CONTACT_DATABASE.csv with new email
Re-send Email 1A with correct address
Note in tracking: "Original bounced, resent to [new email]"
```

### Issue: Zero opens after 5 days

**Possible causes:**
```
□ Email in spam folder
□ Subject line not compelling
□ Bad email list quality
□ Undo Send was clicked (oops)
```

**Action:**
```
□ Create new subject line variation
□ Send Email 1B early (urgency)
□ Add company-specific detail
Example subject: "URGENT: Precision Ag Grant deadline Q3"
```

### Issue: Email opened but no click

**Diagnosis:**
```
□ Subject was compelling (they opened)
□ Body/CTA not compelling enough (they didn't click)
```

**Action:**
```
□ Follow-up with calendar invite to call
□ Don't just resend same email
□ Example: "Hey Michelle, saw you opened my email. 
  Can we grab 15 min Thursday?"
```

### Issue: Response says "wrong person"

**Example response:**
```
"Hi, Michelle forwarded me your note but she's not the right contact. 
You should reach out to [Name] in Strategic Planning."
```

**Action (GOOD SIGN!):**
```
✅ You have a warm intro
□ Send Email 1A to new contact
□ Reference: "Thanks to [original contact] for the intro"
□ Don't resend template — personalize for new person
```
