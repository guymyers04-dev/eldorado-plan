## PART 1: FEDERAL GRANTS & LOAN PROGRAMS

### USDA PROGRAMS (RURAL DEVELOPMENT)

#### 1. REAP (Renewable Energy for Agriculture Program)
- **Agency:** USDA (Rural Development)
- **Amount:** 25% grant + 75% loan (max $500K grant)
- **Eligibility:** Agricultural operations, rural businesses
- **Application Deadline:** Varies (annual, typically spring)
- **Contact:** USDA RD Kansas (816-926-6301)
- **Link:** rd.usda.gov/reap
- **El Dorado Fit:** Renewable energy infrastructure at mega-site, ag facilities

#### 2. VAPG (Value-Added Producer Grants)
- **Agency:** USDA (Rural Development)
- **Amount:** Up to $250K (50% cost share)
- **Eligibility:** Agricultural producers adding value (processing, marketing, ag-tech)
- **Application Deadline:** Varies (typically Jan/Feb for federal fiscal year)
- **Contact:** Kansas Department of Agriculture (785-296-3700)
- **Link:** usda.gov/programs-services/value-added-producer-grants
- **El Dorado Fit:** Food manufacturing hub, ag-tech companies, AMAZONE supply chain

#### 3. Community Facilities Loans & Grants
- **Agency:** USDA (Rural Development)
- **Amount:** Loans up to $40M (grants rare, max $50K–$250K)
- **Eligibility:** Community buildings, public infrastructure, water, sewer
- **Application Timeline:** Rolling; process 3–6 months
- **Contact:** USDA RD Kansas (816-926-6301)
- **Link:** rd.usda.gov/programs-services/community-facilities-loans-grants
- **El Dorado Fit:** Water infrastructure expansion, wastewater treatment, broadband

#### 4. ReConnect Broadband Loans & Grants
- **Agency:** USDA (Rural Development)
- **Amount:** Loans + grants (grants: 40–60% of project cost, max $50M)
- **Eligibility:** Broadband infrastructure in underserved rural areas
- **Application Deadline:** 2024 deadline closed; 2025 round expected late 2024
- **Contact:** USDA RD Kansas (816-926-6301)
- **Link:** rd.usda.gov/programs-services/reconnect-broadband
- **El Dorado Fit:** Last-mile broadband to mega-site, industrial areas

#### 5. Water & Waste Disposal Loans
- **Agency:** USDA (Rural Development)
- **Amount:** Loans up to $11M (favorable terms: 1.375% interest)
- **Eligibility:** Public/non-profit water/wastewater systems
- **Application Deadline:** Rolling
- **Contact:** USDA RD Kansas (816-926-6301)
- **Link:** rd.usda.gov/programs-services/water-waste-disposal-loans-grants
- **El Dorado Fit:** Water treatment expansion, wastewater plant upgrades

#### 6. RBDG (Rural Business Development Grants)
- **Agency:** USDA (Rural Development)
- **Amount:** Up to $500K (for feasibility studies, business plans)
- **Eligibility:** Rural businesses, nonprofits, economic development orgs
- **Application Deadline:** Varies (typically fall)
- **Contact:** USDA RD Kansas (816-926-6301)
- **Link:** rd.usda.gov/programs-services/rural-business-development-grants
- **El Dorado Fit:** Business planning for new ventures (food hall incubator, hotel, innovation campus)

### HUD (HOUSING & URBAN DEVELOPMENT) PROGRAMS

#### 15. CDBG (Community Development Block Grants)
- **Agency:** HUD
- **Amount:** Annual allocation (~$200K–$2M per city, varies by formula)
- **Eligibility:** Community development projects (housing, infrastructure, economic dev)
- **Application Deadline:** Annual (varies by state; typically spring)
- **Contact:** Kansas Department of Commerce (KDHE) — administers CDBG pool
- **Link:** hud.gov/program-offices/public-indian-housing/cdbg
- **El Dorado Fit:** Downtown revitalization, affordable housing, water infrastructure

#### 16. LIHTC (Low-Income Housing Tax Credit)
- **Agency:** HUD (administered through state)
- **Amount:** 4% or 9% annual tax credits (9% = ~$10–$15M financing per 100-unit development)
- **Eligibility:** Affordable multifamily housing (at least 20 years affordability)
- **Application Deadline:** Annual (typically summer; varies by state)
- **Contact:** Kansas Housing Resources Board (KHRB) (785-296-3085)
- **Link:** hud.gov/lihtc
- **El Dorado Fit:** Workforce housing development (80–100 unit LIHTC project: $12–18M financing)

#### 17. HUD Section 108 Loan Guarantee Program
- **Agency:** HUD
- **Amount:** Loans (up to 5× annual CDBG allocation; unsecured, favorable terms)
- **Eligibility:** Economic development projects (repaid from tax revenue)
- **Application Deadline:** Rolling (SCKEDD can facilitate)
- **Contact:** SCKEDD / HUD regional office
- **Link:** hud.gov/section-108
- **El Dorado Fit:** Downtown revitalization, hotel development, incubator construction (repaid via tax increment)

#### 18. Community Facilities Loans (HUD)
- **Agency:** HUD (separate from USDA)
- **Amount:** Loans up to $40M (unsecured, favorable terms for nonprofits)
- **Eligibility:** Community development nonprofits
- **Application Deadline:** Rolling
- **Contact:** HUD regional office
- **Link:** hud.gov/programs/oci/community-facilities
- **El Dorado Fit:** YMCA expansion, senior care facilities, community centers

#### 19. CCBHC (Certified Community Behavioral Health Centers) Expansion
- **Agency:** HHS (Health & Human Services)
- **Amount:** Grants ($1–$2M per center)
- **Eligibility:** Behavioral health / mental health centers serving rural underserved areas
- **Application Deadline:** Annual (varies)
- **Contact:** SAMHSA (Substance Abuse & Mental Health Services Admin) (1-800-662-HELP)
- **Link:** samhsa.gov/grants/ccbhc-expansion
- **El Dorado Fit:** Behavioral health clinic (part of health economy strategy)

## PART 3: FEDERAL TAX INCENTIVES & CREDITS

### GENERAL BUSINESS CREDITS

#### 41. Work Opportunity Tax Credit (WOTC)
- **Status:** Active (federal)
- **Amount:** Tax credit per new hire ($2,400–$9,600 per employee)
- **Eligibility:** Hiring from targeted groups (disabled, ex-felons, long-term unemployed, etc.)
- **Application:** Employer applies to IRS
- **Contact:** IRS (irs.gov)
- **Link:** irs.gov/credits-deductions/work-opportunity-tax-credit
- **El Dorado Fit:** Every niche industry facility hiring from El Dorado labor force qualifies; significant aggregate tax savings

#### 42. Opportunity Zone Capital Gains Deferral
- **Status:** Active (federal TCJA 2017)
- **Amount:** Tax deferral + 15% exclusion on capital gains if invested in OZ for 10 years
- **Eligibility:** Capital gains invested in designated Opportunity Zone census tracts
- **Application:** Investment registration; automatic incentive
- **El Dorado Fit:** El Dorado downtown tract (Census 2010 designated) qualifies; hotel, food hall, innovation campus projects eligible

#### 43. Historic Tax Credit (HTC)
- **Status:** Active (federal + Kansas state stack)
- **Amount:** 20% federal + 25% Kansas (45% combined of qualified project costs)
- **Eligibility:** Listed on National Register of Historic Places (NRHP)
- **Application:** NRHP nomination + project approval from State Historic Preservation Office (SHPO)
- **Contact:** Kansas SHPO (785-272-8681)
- **Link:** nps.gov/tps/tax-incentives
- **El Dorado Fit:** Central Avenue NRHP district + historic adaptive reuse (boutique hotel, office conversion, etc.)

#### 44. New Markets Tax Credit (NMTC)
- **Status:** Active (federal)
- **Amount:** 39% tax credit on $100K investment = $39K credit (leverages CDFI financing)
- **Eligibility:** Projects in low-income census tracts, served by CDFI
- **Application:** CDFI (Rural Development Partners has $90M allocation as of Dec 2025)
- **Contact:** Rural Development Partners / CDFI Fund (treasurydirect.gov/nmtc)
- **Link:** cdfi.treasury.gov
- **El Dorado Fit:** Boutique hotel ($5–8M), food hall incubator ($3–4M), innovation campus ($8–12M) — Downtown Opportunity Zone census tract

### ENERGY & ENVIRONMENTAL CREDITS

#### 45. Energy Investment Tax Credit (ITC)
- **Status:** Active (federal)
- **Amount:** 30% credit on solar, wind, geothermal installation (capped for individuals; no cap for businesses)
- **Eligibility:** Renewable energy systems
- **Application:** Automatic; claimed on tax return
- **Contact:** IRS / Energy.gov
- **Link:** energy.gov/investing-clean-energy
- **El Dorado Fit:** Solar installations on city buildings, mega-site facilities, water treatment plant, renewable energy corridor projects

#### 46. Production Tax Credit (PTC)
- **Status:** Active (federal)
- **Amount:** Per-kWh credit (wind: ~$26/kWh; solar: varies)
- **Eligibility:** Renewable energy generation facilities
- **Application:** Ongoing; project registration with IRS
- **Contact:** IRS / Energy.gov
- **Link:** energy.gov
- **El Dorado Fit:** Renewable energy corridor (solar/wind development on mega-site)

#### 47. CHIPS Act Tax Credits (Federal)
- **Status:** Active (2022–ongoing)
- **Amount:** 10–30% federal tax credits on semiconductor + battery manufacturing capex
- **Eligibility:** Domestic semiconductor or advanced battery facilities
- **Application:** Company applies to IRS
- **Contact:** IRS / Energy.gov
- **Link:** commerce.gov/chips-america
- **El Dorado Fit:** Advanced battery supply chain facilities (Molicel, Xylem, Recycled Battery Materials) — potentially 10–30% CAPEX credit

#### 48. Clean Hydrogen Production Tax Credit
- **Status:** Active (2022–ongoing)
- **Amount:** Up to $3/kg of hydrogen (production ITC)
- **Eligibility:** Hydrogen production facilities
- **Application:** Company applies
- **Contact:** IRS / Energy.gov
- **Link:** energy.gov/hydrogen
- **El Dorado Fit:** Advanced manufacturing energy infrastructure; potential hydrogen generation for industrial use
