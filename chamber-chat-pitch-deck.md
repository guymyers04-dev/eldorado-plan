## SPEAKER'S NOTES (What to Say)

### OPENING (30 seconds)
*"Good morning, Chamber friends. I'm here today to talk about something that impacts every business in this room: how El Dorado attracts talent, investment, and growth. We've done amazing work—the Chamber, Main Street, City government—but we're working in silos. Vision 2040 is about breaking those silos and creating one unified platform where we can all do more, together."*

### SLIDE 2 TALKING POINTS (60 seconds)
*"Think about what we have right now:*
- *1,559 Chamber member businesses—that's incredible reach*
- *2,000+ students coming out of USD 490 every year—that's our next generation of talent and business owners*
- *160+ nonprofits and civic organizations—that's organizing capacity*

*The problem? These groups aren't talking to each other. A student doesn't know about internships. An investor doesn't see the deal flow. A Main Street entrepreneur doesn't know about business development resources.*

*Vision 2040 fixes that. It's a single place where all these groups can connect, collaborate, and accelerate growth."*

### SLIDE 3 TALKING POINTS (60 seconds)
*"Vision 2040 is a digital platform—kind of like a combination of LinkedIn, AngelList, and a community forum, all built specifically for El Dorado.*

*It connects five critical groups:*
- *Businesses—you—who want market intelligence and talent*
- *Investors looking for deal flow and returns*
- *Young professionals who want careers and community*
- *Students who need opportunities and mentorship*
- *Community members who want to be heard and engaged*

*The platform has three layers. Intelligence: real-time data on jobs, properties, market trends. Activation: events, partnerships, community input. Scaling: apps and integrations that make it all work seamlessly."*

### SLIDE 4 TALKING POINTS (90 seconds)
*"Why now? Four reasons:*

**First: The Housing Boom.** Main Street is investing $750K in revitalization. We have the 124 Lofts project downtown, ROOMS grant funding. Momentum is real. But we're not marketing these opportunities. There's no investor pipeline. No one knows these units are coming. Vision 2040 changes that.

**Second: Job Growth.** We've identified 243 in-demand jobs across precision ag, logistics, clean tech. That's $3.5–6.7M in annual tax revenue—but only if we can fill those jobs and attract talent. Right now, young people are leaving El Dorado for Emporia or Wichita because they don't see opportunity. Vision 2040 shows them the opportunity.

**Third: We're losing talent.** Emporia hired a Chief Innovation Officer. They have a startup accelerator. Andover has co-working spaces. We're the best-positioned city in Butler County—better schools, better cost of living, better lake—but we're not marketing it. Vision 2040 positions El Dorado as THE destination for young professionals in Kansas.

**Fourth: Business burnout.** I talk to business owners all the time who care deeply about El Dorado but don't have time to participate in every committee, every board, every initiative. Vision 2040 gives you one place to stay connected, one way to contribute, one channel for information."*

### SLIDE 5 TALKING POINTS (60 seconds)
*"As a Chamber member, here's what you get:*

*Market Intelligence—real-time data on what jobs are opening, what skills are in demand, where investment is happening, what your competitors are doing.*

*Investor Access—if you're looking for capital, you see a pipeline of active deals. If you're an investor, you see opportunities.*

*Talent Pipeline—direct connection to 2,000+ students, internship program, skills marketplace. You can hire trained people on day one.*

*Network Amplification—we market Vision 2040 to 1,500+ Chamber members. So when you participate, you're seen and heard by the entire business community. And you get access to co-marketing—we amplify your message."*

### SLIDE 6 TALKING POINTS (60 seconds)
*"We're not waiting for November to launch. We're starting TODAY. This month, we're running:*

*A Chamber co-marketing campaign—your newsletter, our social media, unified messaging.*

*An Independence Day booth on July 4 with 2,000+ families—conversations, materials, recruitment.*

*A Butler County Fair education bootcamp in mid-July with 5,000–10,000 attendees—four rotating stations teaching about housing, jobs, business, and vision.*

*Plus farmers markets, Instagram takeovers, TikTok shorts, ambassador recruitment.*

*By Labor Day, we want 2,000–5,000 qualified leads, 100–200 ambassadors, and the momentum to launch the full platform in November."*

### SLIDE 7 TALKING POINTS (90 seconds)
*"Here's where you come in. There are three ways to help:*

**First: Become a Vision 2040 Ambassador.** This is for business leaders and influencers. 30 minutes a month. We give you talking points, social graphics, and exclusive data. You share it with your network. You get early access to deals and job data. And honestly? It's leadership. You're publicly saying 'I believe in El Dorado's future.'*

**Second: Sponsor an event.** If you want brand visibility and community goodwill, sponsor July 4 or the County Fair or our monthly First Fridays pop-ups. $250 to $1,500 depending on the event. You get logo placement, social media shout-outs, networking.*

**Third: Partner on business development.** If you want to shape El Dorado's economic future, lead recruitment in your sector. Tech sector leaders could host a roundtable and recruit tech talent. Logistics companies could market El Dorado's US-77 opportunity. You become a thought leader and get first look at deal flow.*

*Pick one, pick all three—however you want to engage."*

### SLIDE 8 TALKING POINTS (45 seconds)
*"Timeline-wise, here's what to expect:*

*July–August: Community activation. We're loud, we're visible, we're building momentum. You'll see Vision 2040 everywhere—at events, on social media, in the news.*

*September–October: We build the actual platform. Data dashboards, tools, integrations. Beta testing with early partners.*

*November: Public launch. Full website, full platform, media campaign, celebration.*

*December and beyond: Sustained growth. Monthly events, webinars, updates. It's not a one-time campaign. It's a living platform."*

### SLIDE 9 TALKING POINTS (45 seconds)
*"How do we know if this is working?*

*Short term—by Labor Day—we want 2,000–5,000 qualified leads, 100–200 ambassadors, 50,000+ social impressions, and 10+ partnership agreements.*

*By end of year, we're looking at 10,000 community members engaged, 500 business decision-makers on the platform, 50+ new housing units committed, 100+ internship placements.*

*By mid-2027, we want 1,500 active users, 250 new jobs created, $50–100M in new investment, and 95%+ community awareness.*

*These aren't pie-in-the-sky numbers. They're based on case studies from Emporia, Eureka Springs, and other comparable towns that have done community activation well."*

### CLOSING (60 seconds)
*"Vision 2040 is about one thing: making El Dorado the place where the best ideas, the best talent, and the best opportunities come together.*

*We're building a platform BY El Dorado FOR El Dorado. Owned by the community, led by business leaders, powered by data.*

*You can wait and see how it goes. Or you can help shape it.*

*I'm asking you to do three things:*
1. *Tell someone about Vision 2040 this week.*
2. *Consider becoming an ambassador or sponsoring an event.*
3. *Come back and join us at the next Chamber Chat to hear how it's going.*

*Thank you."*
