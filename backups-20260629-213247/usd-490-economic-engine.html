Why USD 490 Matters for Economic Development

⭐ Family Retention

Excellent schools are the #1 reason families relocate. Strong USD 490 = stronger ability to retain young families and attract remote workers with children.

⭐ Workforce Pipeline

CTE programs produce certified welders, nurses, electricians, mechanics. Direct pipeline to local employers (Halliburton, hospitals, manufacturing).

⭐ Community Anchor

Hub for events, sports, activities, civic gatherings. Strongest institutional voice in community development & regional partnerships.

Education-Industry Alignment

Workforce Development: Filling Regional Skills Gaps

Vision 2040 positions USD 490's CTE programs as a regional training center for high-demand sectors: healthcare, advanced manufacturing, skilled trades, logistics, and renewable energy. Strategic alignment with employer needs ensures graduate employment and higher earning potential.

Current Career & Technical Education (CTE) Programs

Advanced Manufacturing

Welding, CAD, CNC machining, industrial maintenance. Prepares students for manufacturing jobs in El Dorado + region.

Employers: Pro Hyperscale, local manufacturers, Wichita shops

Healthcare Careers

Nursing assistant, phlebotomy, medical coding. Feeder to Comanche County Hospital and regional healthcare networks.

Employers: CCH, Wichita medical centers, regional clinics

Skilled Trades

HVAC, electrical, plumbing, construction. Supports residential + commercial development pipeline.

Employers: Local contractors, construction companies

Agriculture & Natural Resources

Crop science, soil management, equipment operation. Bridges traditional economy with sustainability initiatives.

Employers: Regional farms, equipment dealers, renewable energy

Business & IT

Data analytics, cybersecurity, digital marketing. Prepares remote workers and tech-enabled entrepreneurs.

Employers: Remote tech companies, local business service firms

Vision 2040 Strategic Expansions

Commercial Driving License (CDL)

Regional logistics hub needs 150+ CDL drivers by 2030. USD 490 CDL training center produces 40-50 certified drivers annually.

Food Processing

Proposed food processing hub needs facility operators, quality technicians, food science specialists. USD 490 adds curriculum track.

Renewable Energy

Wind/solar technician certification. Aligns with regional clean energy growth and county sustainability goals.

Advanced Robotics

Prepares students for automation-heavy manufacturing and logistics environments. Future-proofs workforce.

Hospitality & Events

Supports new tourism infrastructure (marinas, resorts, dining). Trains event management, food service leadership.

Entrepreneurship

Business plan competition, startup incubator partnership. Supports remote worker retention + Makers Guild expansion.

Ecosystem Alignment

Regional Partnerships & Economic Alignment

Vision 2040 strengthens USD 490's role as a regional education leader through strategic partnerships with employers, higher ed, and community organizations. These partnerships ensure CTE programs meet real workforce demand.

Employer Partnerships

  • Pro Hyperscale: Manufacturing equipment, instructor training, equipment donations
  • Comanche County Hospital: Healthcare pathway development, clinical placements
  • Local Construction: Skilled trades equipment, apprenticeship partnerships
  • Logistics Operators: CDL curriculum development, driver recruitment
  • Regional Tech Companies: IT/cybersecurity curriculum, internships

Higher Education Pathways

  • Wichita State University: Dual enrollment, engineering pipeline
  • Kansas State University: Agriculture, renewable energy programs
  • Butler Community College: 2-year degree pathways, reverse transfer
  • Vocational Certifications: Industry-recognized credentials (NCCER, ASE, etc.)

Community & Municipal Alignment

  • El Dorado Inc.: Business recruitment, mentor partnerships
  • City of El Dorado: Capital funding, economic development coordination
  • Chamber of Commerce: Networking events, employer showcases
  • Makers Guild: Entrepreneurship, maker space access
  • Butler County Economic Dev.: Regional workforce strategy alignment
