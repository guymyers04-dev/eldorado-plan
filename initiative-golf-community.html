Initiative Overview

What Is It?

Develop 230-315 residential units (estates, townhomes, apartments) integrated with championship golf course, resort amenities, and community centers. Anchors a complete golf lifestyle destination.

Why It Matters

Golf communities attract high-net-worth residents, snowbirds, and active adults willing to invest in quality of life. Drives property values, tax base, and quality retail/dining development. Positions El Dorado as lifestyle destination.

Market Positioning

Golf communities attract three primary markets:

Regional Affluent Retirees

Wichita + Kansas City metro professionals retiring to smaller city with quality of life. Age 60+, $1M+ net worth target.

Seasonal Residents (Snowbirds)

Winter-month residents from upper midwest/northeast. Second homes attracting $250-400K average price points.

Active Adults & Executives

Executive transfer market + relocation from bigger cities seeking affordable upscale lifestyle. Ages 45-65.
