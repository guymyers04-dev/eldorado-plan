📋 Implementation Checklist

Step 1: Get Your Measurement ID

Action: Create a Google Analytics 4 property (if you don't have one)

  1. Go to analytics.google.com
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Click "Create Property"
  4. Name it "El Dorado Vision 2040"
  5. Set timezone to "America/Chicago"
  6. Note your Measurement ID (format: G-XXXXXXXXXX)

Step 2: Search & Replace in Project

Replace this placeholder with your actual Measurement ID:

G-PLACEHOLDER-ID

In your editor, use Find & Replace:

  • Find: G-PLACEHOLDER-ID
  • Replace: G-XXXXXXXXXX (your actual ID)
  • Replace All: across the entire project

Step 3: Verify Implementation

Verify GA4 is working:

  1. Upload the project to your web server (or serve locally)
  2. Visit your El Dorado website
  3. Go to Google Analytics → Real-time report
  4. You should see yourself as an active user
  5. Click through pages and watch the dashboard update in real-time

Step 4: Set Up Dashboards (Optional but Recommended)

Create custom dashboards for tracking Phase 10 success:

  • Executive Dashboard: Sessions, users, top pages, session duration
  • Content Performance: Page views, scroll depth, time on page
  • Engagement Tools: Development Finder, Investor Portal, Project Tracker usage
  • Conversions: Form submissions, inquiry rate, lead quality

❓ FAQ

Q: Does GA4 slow down my website?

A: No. GA4 is optimized for performance and loads asynchronously (doesn't block page rendering).

Q: How long before I see data?

A: Real-time reports show data within seconds. Full reports update within 24 hours.

Q: Can I track form submissions?

A: Yes. See GOOGLE_ANALYTICS_4_SETUP.md for custom event setup instructions.

Q: Is my visitor data private?

A: GA4 is GDPR-compliant and doesn't track personally identifiable information without consent.
