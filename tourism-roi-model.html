Key Drivers & Assumptions

  • Conservative (Golf Only): 9-hole course + minimal lodging adds ~27K visitors/yr
  • Moderate (Lake + Golf): Marina + events + 50-room hotel adds ~50K visitors/yr
  • Aggressive (Full Vision): All assets + RV park + boutique spa adds ~95K visitors/yr by 2031
  • Regional draw: Assumes 60–70% of growth comes from 100-mile region (Wichita, OKC, Tulsa)
  • Seasonality: Projections account for summer peak (45% of annual), spring/fall (40%), winter (15%)
  • Multiplier effect: Each direct job generates ~1.2 indirect jobs in supporting services
Sources: ESRI Tourism Analytics, PGA Tour Economic Impact Studies, Hotel Management Benchmarks, STR Global Hotel Performance Data, El Dorado Vision 2040 Strategic Initiatives
