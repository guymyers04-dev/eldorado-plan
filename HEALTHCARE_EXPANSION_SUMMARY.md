### 4. Risk Mitigation & Success Metrics

**Section Location:** Lines 1019-1107 (89 lines)

Identifies four critical risks with specific mitigation strategies:

**Risk 1: Low Surgical Case Volume**
- Problem: ASC needs 12-15 procedures/day for sustainability
- Mitigation:
  - Pre-contract 5-7 specialists BEFORE ASC opens (2-year commitment)
  - Regional marketing targeting rural Kansas surgical demand
  - Physician recruitment bonus pool: $500K-$1M (first 3 years)

**Risk 2: Behavioral Health Capacity Underutilization**
- Problem: 24-bed inpatient facility requires 80%+ occupancy
- Mitigation:
  - Start with 12-bed pilot (expandable)
  - Mobile crisis team + outpatient services capture referrals
  - SAMHSA grant requirements mandate 75%+ uninsured coverage (limited financial risk)

**Risk 3: Physician Recruitment Failure**
- Problem: Rural physician recruitment is notoriously difficult
- Mitigation:
  - Offer $80K-$120K combined loan repayment packages
  - Practice buy-in assistance (20% down payment subsidy)
  - Recruit retired specialists as part-time mentors

**Risk 4: Market Cannibalization from Wichita**
- Problem: Wichita health systems may extract profit without local reinvestment
- Mitigation:
  - Prioritize regional partnerships
  - Keep SBA Hospital locally-owned (no sale to Wichita systems)
  - Local physician hiring guarantees profit reinvestment
