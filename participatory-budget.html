💡 How Quadratic Voting Works

  • 1st vote: Costs 1 point (1² = 1)
  • 2nd vote: Costs 4 points total (2² = 4)
  • 3rd vote: Costs 9 points total (3² = 9)
  • 4th vote: Costs 16 points total (4² = 16)
  • 5th vote: Costs 25 points total (5² = 25)

This gives your passion a voice — show how much you care about each project by voting multiple times. You have 15 points to distribute.
