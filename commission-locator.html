💡 Tips for Commissioner Engagement

Be Specific

Reference the exact Vision 2040 project you're commenting on.

Be Personal

Share how the project affects you and your neighborhood.

Be Respectful

Maintain a professional tone even if you disagree.

Be Brief

City Commission allows 2 minutes per public speaker.

Follow Up

Email your commissioner after the meeting with additional thoughts.

Come Prepared

Bring 5-10 copies of your statement for commissioners and staff.
