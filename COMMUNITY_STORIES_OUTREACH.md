## 🎤 STEP 5: Interview Guide

**Use this structure for each interview:**

### Interview Outline (30 minutes)

**Opening (2 min):**
- "Thanks for taking time to share your story"
- "We're featuring you on El Dorado Vision 2040 — the community's development platform"
- "I want to capture your authentic journey"

**Background (5 min):**
Q1: "Tell me about your background — where are you from, what was your career path?"
Q2: "What was your life like before El Dorado?"

**The Decision (5 min):**
Q3: "What brought you to El Dorado?" OR "What made you decide to come back?"
Q4: "What was it like when you first arrived?"

**Life Here (8 min):**
Q5: "Tell me about your business/career here. What do you do?"
Q6: "How has El Dorado helped you succeed?"
Q7: "What do you love most about living here?"
Q8: "What surprised you most about the community?"

**Impact & Advice (8 min):**
Q9: "What's your impact on the community? (Jobs created, revenue, etc.)"
Q10: "What would you tell someone considering moving to El Dorado?"
Q11: "What does the future look like for you here?"

**Wrap (2 min):**
- "Can we use your first name & photo in the profile?"
- "Any closing thoughts?"
- "We'll send you a draft to review before we publish"

**Recording:**
- [ ] Ask permission to record/take notes
- [ ] Use your phone voice memo or Zoom recording
- [ ] Send transcript request if possible
