## RISK TRIGGERS & CONTINGENCY ACTIVATION

### Staffing Risk
- **Trigger:** DC Director hire not completed by July 31
- **Response (48 hrs):** Engage interim recruiter; extend search timeline to August 31
- **Contingency Plan:** Use interim contractor (Chief Development Officer from similar city) through October

### Prospect Pipeline Risk
- **Trigger:** <5 qualified data center prospects by August 31
- **Response (1 week):** Escalate to backup brokers (Colliers, Jones Lang LaSalle)
- **Contingency Plan:** Pivot to industrial/flex facility prospects (alternative revenue model)

### Financing Risk
- **Trigger:** Housing financing denied Q3 applications
- **Response (1 week):** Activate CDFI pre-applications (Rural Development Partners)
- **Contingency Plan:** Reduce Phase 1 unit count to 50-75 units; scale back capex

### Market Risk
- **Trigger:** Data center market shows signs of softening (M&A reduction, capex delays)
- **Response (30 days):** Diversify prospect pipeline; increase FDI + hospitality focus
- **Contingency Plan:** Shift capital to housing + lake if data center stalls 6+ months
