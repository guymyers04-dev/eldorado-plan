What is an Ambassador?

A Vision 2040 Ambassador is a trusted community leader who helps spread the word about Vision 2040 to their personal and professional networks.

You're not selling anything. You're sharing opportunities and inviting people to explore El Dorado's future.

Your Commitment

30 Minutes/Month

We provide all materials. You share them with your networks whenever it fits your schedule.

What You Get

5 Exclusive Benefits

  • Early access to job forecasts and skills marketplace
  • First look at property development pipeline and deals
  • Monthly branded toolkit (slides, graphics, talking points)
  • Optional monthly training calls with city leadership
  • Community recognition and newsletter features

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't have time for the monthly call? +

No problem! The monthly training call is completely optional. You get all the materials via email regardless. Just share what resonates with you, when you want.

How do I promote Vision 2040? +

We give you everything you need: 15 social media graphics (post weekly), slide deck (share in meetings), 1-pagers (print & hand out), email templates (forward to your contacts), and talking points (use in conversations). Choose what works for your networks.

Will I have to sell anything? +

No. You're just sharing information about opportunities in El Dorado and inviting people to learn more. It's more like being an enthusiastic friend than a salesperson.

When is the first training call? +

August 15, 2026 at 6 PM CT (Zoom). We'll brief you on Vision 2040 data, give you talking points, teach you storytelling techniques, and answer all your questions. Recording available if you can't attend live.

Can multiple people from my company be ambassadors? +

Yes! Invite your team. The more ambassadors you have, the more networks you reach. We've had entire leadership teams sign up.

What if I want to do more than just be an ambassador? +

Great! Many ambassadors also sponsor events or become business development partners. You can do as much or as little as you want. Check out the sponsorship and partnership pages for other ways to get involved.

Still have questions? +

Contact us! Email info@eldorado.gov or call (316) 321-9100. We're happy to answer any questions before you commit.
