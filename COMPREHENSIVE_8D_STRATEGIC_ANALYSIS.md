## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

El Dorado Vision 2040 is a 14-year economic transformation strategy (2026–2040) leveraging three unique competitive advantages:

1. **Water Rights:** 300M gallons/day owned capacity (ONLY in region; 567% above benchmark)
2. **I-35 Direct Access:** Exits 71/76 on Interstate 35 (29 miles to Wichita metro, 651K population)
3. **Mega-Site Infrastructure:** 1,131-acre pre-zoned industrial park, shovel-ready by 2027

**Target Transformation:**
- Population: 4,500 (2026) → 10,000–15,000 (2030–2040)
- Annual Tax Base: $12M–$16M (2026) → $50M–$86M (2030)
- Employment: +1,130–1,920 jobs (Phase 1 by 2030)
- Regional Role: Wichita metro growth engine + I-35 corridor data center hub

**Investment Required:** $680M–$1.3B (Phase 1, 2026–2030)  
**ROI:** 3.3x–5.5x tax revenue multiplier  
**Timeline:** 10-year buildout to full maturity (2040)
